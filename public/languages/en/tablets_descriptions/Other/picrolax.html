INDICATIONS

Picrolax is a himalaya herbal formulation used for the treatment of constipation. Picrolax works as a safe and gentle laxative.

Picrolax can be used to treat:

acute sporadic constipation
constipation associated with hemorrhoids
constipation in pre- and postoperative conditions and in cardiac patients

INSTRUCTIONS

Follow the directions for using this medicine provided by your doctor. Take your medicine exactly as directed.

DOSAGE

The usual recommended dosage is 2 capsules taken at bedtime.

STORAGE 

Store at the room temperature away from moisture and sunlight. Keep out of reach of children.

SAFETY INFORMATION

Do not take this medication if you are allergic to its ingredients.

SIDE EFFECTS

Picrolax is not known to have any side effects when taken as prescribed.
