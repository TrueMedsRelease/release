#TOP_TAG##TITLE_OPEN_TAG#What is Famvir?#TITLE_CLOSE_TAG# #BLOCK_OPEN_TAG# Famvir is an antiviral drug. It slows the growth and spread of the herpes virus so that the body can fight off the infection. Famvir will not cure herpes, but it can lessen the symptoms of the infections.
Famvir is used to treat infections caused by herpes viruses. Illnesses caused by herpes viruses include genital herpes, cold sores, shingles, and chicken pox. #BLOCK_CLOSE_TAG# #TITLE_OPEN_TAG#Important information about Famvir#TITLE_CLOSE_TAG# #BLOCK_OPEN_TAG# Take this medication for the entire length of time prescribed by your doctor. Your symptoms may get better before the infection is completely treated.
Treatment with Famvir should be started as soon as possible after the first appearance of symptoms (such as tingling, burning, blisters).
Herpes infections are contagious and you can infect other people, even while you are being treated with Famvir. Avoid letting infected areas come into contact with other people. Avoid touching an infected area and then touching your eyes. Wash your hands frequently to prevent passing the infection to others. #BLOCK_CLOSE_TAG# #TITLE_OPEN_TAG#Before taking Famvir#TITLE_CLOSE_TAG# #BLOCK_OPEN_TAG# Do not take this medication if you are allergic to penciclovir (Denavir). Do not take the medication in larger amounts, or take it for longer than recommended by your doctor.
Treatment with Famvir should be started as soon as possible after the first appearance of symptoms (such as tingling, burning, blisters).
Take each dose with a full glass of water. Drink plenty of water while you are taking this medication to keep your kidneys working properly.
Famvir can be taken with or without food. Taking Famvir with food may decrease stomach upset.
Take this medication for the entire length of time prescribed by your doctor. Your symptoms may get better before the infection is completely treated. Famvir will not treat a viral infection such as the common cold or flu.
Lesions caused by herpes viruses should be kept as clean and dry as possible. Wearing loose clothing may help to prevent irritation of the lesions.

Famvir will not prevent the spread of genital herpes.
