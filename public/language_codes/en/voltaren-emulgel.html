Common use

Voltaren Emulgel is used for supporting joint and muscle health by managing musculoskeletal pain, arthritis, strains, sprains and inflammation. It contains Diclofenac Diethylamine (1%), which may help reduce pain and swelling by blocking the chemical messenger responsible for inflammation. This gel helps in managing low back pain, post-traumatic pain and other conditions affecting muscles, joints, tendons and ligaments.

Dosage and direction

Clean and dry the affected area before applying the gel. Take a small amount of Voltaren Emulgel and gently massage it into the skin until fully absorbed. Apply 3 to 4 times daily or as directed by a healthcare professional.

Precautions

For external use only. Do not apply on broken or irritated skin to avoid unwanted reactions.
Wash hands thoroughly after use to reduce accidental contact with eyes and mouth. Avoid contact with eyes and mucous membranes. If accidental contact occurs, rinse thoroughly with water.
Not suitable for children under 12 years. Consult a doctor before use in children or elderly individuals with sensitive skin.

Contraindications

Voltaren Emulgel is contraindicated in patients with known allergies to Diclofenac or other NSAIDs. If you experience any allergic reactions after using the product, discontinue if redness/irritation occurs.
It should not be used on open wounds, infections, or broken skin.

Possible side effect

Mild skin irritation, redness, dryness of the skin or itching may occur in some individuals. Discontinue use if severe reactions develop.

Drug interaction

Voltaren Emulgel may interact with other topical NSAIDs (like ketoprofen), oral NSAIDs (such as ibuprofen), and anticoagulants (like warfarin). These interactions could affect the effectiveness and increase the risk of side effects.
Can be used in combination with oral pain relievers if prescribed by a doctor.

Missed dose

If you forget to apply Voltaren Emulgel at the scheduled time, apply it as soon as you remember. However, if it is almost time for your next application, skip the missed dose and continue with your regular schedule.

Overdose

An overdose of Voltaren Emulgel might lead to excessive redness, burning, or irritation at the application site. If these symptoms occur, washing the area with cool water and avoiding further application is advised until speaking with a doctor.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry and dark place. Temperature not exceeding 30 degree celsius. Keep away from direct sunlight and moisture. Keep out of reach of children. Do not freeze.
