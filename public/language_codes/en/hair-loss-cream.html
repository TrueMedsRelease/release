Common use

Hair Loss Cream is used for supporting the healthy growth of hair. It is formulated with botanical ingredients that help improve hair follicular density and strengthen hair strands. The cream works by encouraging the multiplication of hair fibers and stimulating the natural hair growth cycle, making it ideal for those looking to manage hair thinning and support overall scalp health.

Dosage and direction

As directed by the healthcare professional. The cream is formulated with botanical ingredients such as Palasha, which is known for its gentle and nourishing nature. However, as with any topical application, it is advisable to perform a patch test before full use, especially if the scalp is sensitive. Cream can be used alongside shampoos, conditioners, and oils, preferably those with mild or natural formulations. However, it is recommended to consult with a healthcare professional.
