Common use

Rizact tablet is a medicine used to manage migraine headaches in adults. It helps relieve migraine symptoms and prevents the attack from getting worse. This medicine improves blood flow in the brain and reduces abnormal muscle activity, which helps reduce the frequency of migraine.

Dosage and direction

The medicine should be taken as advised by your doctor. It should be swallowed whole with or without food. It's important to take it at the same time every day to maintain a consistent level of the medicine in your body. Make sure to complete the full course of treatment, even if you start feeling better.

Precautions

Remember to consult your doctor before taking Rizact tablet, especially if you have severe kidney or liver disease or if you have certain heart conditions. Pregnant or breastfeeding women should also seek medical advice before using this medicine.

Contraindications

Severe kidney disease: Adjust dosage as directed by the doctor.
Severe liver disease: Adjust dosage as directed by the doctor.
Heart problems: Inform your doctor before taking Rizact.
This medicine is not recommended for use in children under 18 years of age.
Inform your doctor about any known allergies before taking Rizact tablet.

Possible side effect

Dizziness: You may feel lightheaded or unsteady at times.
Dry mouth: Your mouth may feel parched or lacking moisture.
Heaviness: You might experience a sensation of weight or pressure in your body.
Jaw pain: You may feel discomfort or soreness in your jaw area.
Nausea: You might feel queasy or have the urge to vomit.

Drug interaction

Antidepressants: Like fluoxetine, sertraline.
Ergot alkaloids: Such as ergotamine, dihydroergotamine.
Monoamine oxidase inhibitors (MAOIs): Like phenelzine, tranylcypromine.
Selective serotonin reuptake inhibitors (SSRIs): Such as fluoxetine, paroxetine.

Missed dose

If you forgot to take a dose of this medicine, take it as soon as you remember. If it's almost time for your next dose, skip the missed one now and resume your regular dosage schedule. Never increase your dosage without consulting your doctor.

Overdose

Taking more than the recommended dose of Rizact tablet can lead to an overdose. Symptoms may include drowsiness, chest/throat/neck or jaw pain, rapid or irregular heartbeat, shortness of breath, vomiting, and headache. If you or someone else takes too much of this product by accident, get immediate medical attention. Contact your doctor or visit a nearby hospital.
