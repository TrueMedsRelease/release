Common use

Tulsi Sleep is used for managing restful sleep, stress, and mental clarity. The ingredients work synergistically to calm the mind, manage focus, and healthy cognitive functions. It is ideal for anyone seeking a natural way to relax and manage sleep quality without the risk of dependency.

Dosage and direction

Steep one tea sachet in hot water for three to five minutes. Drink one or two cups daily, especially before bedtime for optimal sleep benefits. Suitable for regular use as part of a daily wellness routine.

Precautions

Not to exceed the recommended daily dosage.
Pregnant or lactating women and people with other medical conditions should consult a doctor before using.

Contraindications

Discontinue use and consult your physician in case of any side effects.
Tulsi Sleep is free from common allergens. However, always check the product label for specific allergen information.

Possible side effect

Side effects are rare when used as directed. If you experience any adverse reactions, discontinue use and consult a healthcare provider.

Drug interaction

Other drugs may affect Tulsi Sleep, including prescription and over-the-counter medicines, vitamins, and herbal products. Tell your doctor about all your current medicines and any medicine you start or stop using.

Missed dose

Use the medicine as soon as you can, but skip the missed dose if it is almost time for your next dose. Do not use two doses at one time.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical attention or call you doctor.

Storage

Store at room temperature. Protect from excessive heat and direct sun. Keep out of the reach of children.
