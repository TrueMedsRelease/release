Common use

Celol is primarily used as a nutritional supplement to treat calcium and Vitamin D3 deficiencies. It strengthens bones, manages conditions like osteoporosis and osteomalacia (rickets), and supports proper muscle function and nerve transmission.

Dosage and direction

Take one capsule daily after a meal or as directed by your doctor.

Precautions

Consult your doctor if you are pregnant, planning a pregnancy or breastfeeding and about all the medicines and supplements you take and all the conditions or diseases you suffer from before starting Celol.

Contraindications

You should not take Celol if you are allergic to any of the components of this supplement; You are suffering from tissue calcification (deposit of calcium in body tissues); You are suffering from malabsorption or have high levels of cholesterol; You are taking any other product containing vitamin D or calcium; You have problems with your heart, kidney or liver; You have any existing medical conditions such as diabetes, hypertension, cancer or any other diseases. Do not take Celol if you have a high level of calcium or vitamin D in the blood, a high level of calcium in urine, kidney stones or are bedridden.

Possible side effect

You may experience constipation, nausea, vomiting and an upset stomach after taking Celol. However, not everyone gets these side effects.

Drug interaction

Talk to your doctor regarding any other medications or supplements and especially, if you are using steroids, medicines used to treat heart disorders, medicines used for thyroid disorders, antacids and certain antibiotics, as these medicines may interfere with the effect of Celol. Never take thyroid medicines and this tablet together.

Missed dose

If you miss a Celol dose, take it as soon as possible. However, if it's close to the time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and continue with your regular schedule.

Overdose

It can be danger for you health, seek emergency medical attention.

Storage

Store Celol in a cool and dry place, protect from direct sunlight, moisture and heat. Keep it away from children and pets.
