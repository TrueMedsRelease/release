Common use

Baycip tablet is an antibiotic used to treat various bacterial infections, including respiratory tract infections, urinary tract infections, skin infections, and gastrointestinal infections. It works by inhibiting bacterial DNA replication, leading to the death of bacteria.

Dosage and direction

Swallow the tablet whole with a glass of water. Do not exceed the recommended dose without consulting your doctor. Make sure to drink plenty of fluids while on this medication to prevent dehydration. Do not lie down for at least 30 minutes after taking the medicine to avoid esophageal irritation.

Precautions

Exercise caution when taking Baycip if you're pregnant. It's wise to consult your doctor before starting this medicine.
It's advised to discuss with your doctor before taking Baycip if you are breastfeeding, as it might affect the nursing infant.
If you have kidney problems, this medicine should be used with caution as it may worsen your condition.
Symptoms of myasthenia gravis, a neuromuscular disorder, may worsen when using this tablet.
If you have a history of QT interval prolongation or other heart rhythm disorders, use this medicine cautiously, as it could exacerbate your condition.

Contraindications

Ciprofloxacin is contraindicated in patients who are allergic to quinolone or fluoroquinolone antibiotics.

Possible side effect

Baycip may cause the following side effects: Nausea; Vomiting; Diarrhea; Abdominal pain; Dizziness; Headache.

Drug interaction

Avoid medications like Tizanidine, sucralfate, antacids, clozapine, cyclosporine, methotrexate, phenytoin, probenecid, ropinirole, sildenafil, warfarin, diuretics, oral diabetics, prednisone, antidepressants, and NSAIDs.

Missed dose

If you forget to take your scheduled dose of Baycip, take it as soon as you remember. If it's almost time for the next dose, skip the missed one and continue with your regular schedule. Never increase your dosage without consulting your doctor.

Overdose

In case of an accidental overdose, you may experience symptoms like dizziness and tremors. If you have taken more than the prescribed dose, inform your doctor.
