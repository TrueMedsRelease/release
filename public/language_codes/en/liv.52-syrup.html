Common use

Liv.52 Syrup is used for promoting liver health by assisting in the detoxification process. This syrup helps maintain optimal liver function and supports digestion. This syrup helps protect the liver against various hepatotoxins. It works to enhance appetite, promote growth, and assist in better digestion contributing to overall wellness. Made with a blend of traditional herbs and modern science, it offers a natural approach to liver support.

Dosage and direction

Follow the dosage instructions as directed by your physician. The recommended dosage is 1 teaspoon (twice a day) for adults and 1/2 a teaspoon (twice a day) for children. You can drink water immediately after taking the syrup.

Precautions

Consult a healthcare professional before use, especially if pregnant, nursing, or taking other medications. Do not exceed the recommended dosage. The product is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.

Contraindications

Liv.52 Syrup is usually safe for pregnant or lactating women, but it is best to consult the doctor before use.
If you are allergic to any ingredient in this syrup, do not use it. Avoid consuming alcohol while taking Himalaya Liv.52 Syrup.

Possible side effect

Himalaya Liv.52 Syrup can cause these side effects: Rectal bleeding; Dizziness; Allergic reactions; Weight gain; Nausea; Vomiting; Diarrhea; Abdominal pain. If you experience any adverse reaction to this product, discontinue use and contact the doctor.

Drug interaction

Consult with your healthcare provider before combining it with other medications, especially if you have underlying health conditions.

Missed dose

If you miss a dose, take it as soon as you remember. If it is almost time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and resume your regular schedule. Do not double the dose to make up for a missed one.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical attention.

Storage

Store it away from sunlight, heat and always close the bottle after each use. Keep away from children.
