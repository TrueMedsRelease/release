Common use

Styplon tablet is an Ayurvedic formulation that helps in managing minor bleeding and supports the healing of wounds. It contains ingredients like Amalaki, Lodhra, Anantmul, Praval Pishti and other natural ingredients, which enhance immunity and promote natural recovery processes.

Dosage and direction

Please remember that every patient and their case is different, so the dosage can be different based on the disease, route of administration, patient's age and medical history. Single Maximum Dose: 2 tablet twice daily. You may use lukewarm water to take Styplon Tablets; it is absolutely safe.

Precautions

Consult a healthcare professional before use if you are pregnant, nursing, or have a medical condition.

Contraindications

Free from common allergens. In case of an allergic reaction, please consult a healthcare provider and stop using it immediately.
The product is generally intended for adult use. Consult a healthcare professional before giving it to children.

Possible side effect

Side effects are rare but consult the product label or a healthcare provider for detailed information.

If you are taking other medications, consult your healthcare provider before using Styplon tablets to avoid potential interactions.
