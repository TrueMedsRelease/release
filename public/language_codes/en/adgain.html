Precautions

It should be used with caution if you have pre-existing kidney or liver diseases, or specific medical conditions like malabsorption disorders. Consult a doctor if pregnant, breastfeeding, or taking other supplements.

Contraindications

Adgain capsules is contraindicated for individuals with known allergies to any of its components (biotin, amino acids, vitamins, minerals).

Possible side effect

Adgain capsules are generally safe hair nutritional supplements, but they may cause mild gastrointestinal side effects like nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, constipation, bloating, or stomach discomfort. Some users might experience rare allergic reactions such as skin rashes or itching, headaches, or dizziness.

Drug interaction

Other drugs may affect Adgain, including prescription and over-the-counter medicines, vitamins, and herbal products. Tell your doctor about all other medicines you use.

Missed dose

Take the supplement as soon as you can, but skip the missed dose if it is almost time for your next dose. Do not take two doses at one time.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical attention.

Storage

Store at room temperature away from moisture and heat. Keep out of reach of the children.
