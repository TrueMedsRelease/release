Common use

Xtandi (enzalutamide) is a prescription hormone therapy used to treat certain types of advanced prostate cancer. It belongs to a class of medicines called androgen receptor inhibitors. Xtandi works by blocking the effects of androgens, such as testosterone, which can help prostate cancer cells grow and spread. By blocking these hormones, Xtandi may help slow cancer progression and delay the worsening of the disease.

Dosage and direction

Take Xtandi exactly as prescribed by your doctor. Take Xtandi once a day. Take it with or without food. Take it at the same time each day. Swallow capsules whole. Do not crush, chew, break, open, or dissolve Xtandi.

Precautions

Before starting Xtandi, tell your doctor if you have ever had: a seizure; a stroke, brain injury, or brain tumor; heart disease or circulation problems; high blood pressure; diabetes; high cholesterol or high triglycerides.
Xtandi is not intended for use in women, but it can cause harm to an unborn baby if either parent is taking it during conception or pregnancy. If your partner can become pregnant, use: a condom, and one other effective form of birth control. Keep using birth control during treatment and for at least 3 months after treatment ends to prevent pregnancy.

Contraindications

You should not take Xtandi if you are allergic to the active ingredient enzalutamide or the inactive ingredients.
Xtandi may increase the risk of seizures, dizziness, and falls.

Possible side effect

Common side effects of Xtandi may include: tiredness or weakness; muscle pain, back pain, or joint pain; hot flashes; constipation; diarrhea; decreased appetite; high blood pressure; headache; bleeding; falls; fractures.

Drug interaction

Xtandi can interact with many prescription drugs, over-the-counter medicines, vitamins, and herbal supplements. Tell your doctor about all medicines you take, including any medicines you start or stop during treatment.

Missed dose

If you miss a dose of Xtandi, take it later the same day as soon as you remember. If it is almost time for your next dose, skip the missed dose. Do not take extra medicine to make up for the missed dose.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical help. An Xtandi overdose may increase the risk of seizure.

Storage

Store at 68°F to 77°F (20°C to 25°C). Keep capsules dry. Store in a tightly closed container. Keep away from heat and moisture. Keep away from children and pets.
