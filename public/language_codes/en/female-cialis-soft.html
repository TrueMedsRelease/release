Common use

Female Cialis Soft is used to treat female sexual arousal disorder (FSAD) and female sexual dysfunction. It provides satisfaction in sexual needs and lasting pleasure. The medicine increases the required genital blood flow, along with vaginal compliance resulting in intensified pleasure during sexual activity.

Dosage and direction

Use the medicine as prescribed by your doctor. Do not exceed the recommended dose. It starts to work after 30-60 minutes after its intake. The effect usually lasts up to 12 to 24 hours.

Precautions

It is preferable not to use Female Cialis Soft with grapefruit juice, alcoholic beverages, tobacco. If you smoke tobacco and take Female Cialis Soft concomitantly this may lead to a condition called NAION (sudden optic nerve-related vision loss).
Inform your doctor before to take Female Cialis Soft if you had coronary artery disease, arrhythmia, angina, heart attack or heart failure, hypo- or hypertension, bleeding, stomach ulcers, diabetes, hyperlipidemia (excess of lipids in the blood), sickle-cell anemia, liver or kidney failure in history.

Contraindications

Do not use Female Cialis Soft if you are allergic to any ingredient; you have been advised by your doctor to avoid sexual activity because of heart problems; you use certain recreational drugs called "poppers" (e.g., amyl nitrate, butyl nitrate); you are taking nitrates (e.g., isosorbide, nitroglycerin) in any form (e.g., tablet, capsule, patch, ointment) or nitroprusside.

Possible side effect

Most usual side effects of Female Cialis Soft are: facial flushing (reddening), headaches, stomach upset, diarrhea, flu-like symptoms and nausea. In more rare cases low blood pressure, blurred vision and changes in color vision. Aggravation of heart problems in patients with pre-existing heart disease using Female Cialis Soft was reported.

Drug interaction

Co-administration with medications containing organic nitrates may cause severe hypotension. Avoid taking Female Cialis Soft with medications such as Erythrityl Tetranitrate, Isosorbide Dinitrate, Isosorbide Mononitrate, Nitroglycerin, Pentaerythritol Tetranitrate, also erythromycin, ketoconazole (Nizoral), itraconazole (Sporanox), indinavir (Crixivan) and ritonavir (Norvir).
Inform your doctor if you are taking Atazanavir, Bunazosin, Clarithromycin, Doxazosin, Erythromycin, Indinavir, Itraconazole, Ketoconazole, Moxisylyte, Nefazodone, Nelfinavir, Phenoxybenzamine, Phentolamine, Prazosin, Saquinavir, Telithromycin, Terazosin, Trimazosin, Urapidil as your Female Cialis Soft dose may require correction.

Missed dose

If you miss a dose of Female Cialis Soft, skip the missed dose. Go back to your regular dosing schedule. Do not take 2 doses at once.

Overdose

Overdose of Female Cialis Soft causes symptoms described in side effects. Seek for immediate medical help if you believe that took too much of this medication.

Storage

Store Female Cialis Soft up to 77 degrees F (25 degrees C). Store away from heat, moisture, and light. Do not store in the bathroom. Keep out of the reach of children and away from pets.
