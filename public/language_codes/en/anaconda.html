Common use

Anaconda is an alternate drug for Sildenafil Citrate and is used to treat the condition of impotence also called Erectile Dysfunction. Anaconda is known to contain sildenafil citrate, which forms one of the major ingredients in the medicine. This ingredient is responsible for improving the erection-forming capacity of a man.

Dosage and direction

It is advised to not take more than one tablet within a time frame of 24 hours. It is an oral drug. You can take this medicine with water. Take it an hour before getting involved with someone sexually.

Precautions

Keep Anaconda away from children below 18 as it should not be used as a recreational drug.
You should not drink or smoke while you have consumed this drug as it can accentuate the side effects shown by this medicine.

Contraindications

You should not consume Anaconda if you are allergic to any of the ingredients of this medicine, whether it is active or non-active.
If you are already taking drugs for diabetes, liver disorder, or hypertension, then you should speak to your doctor before consuming this drug.
If you are taking this drug with nitrates, you should not consume this medicine as it can cause an overdose of this medicine.

Possible side effect

Common side effects: Headache; Nose Bleeding; Flushing of the face; Upset stomach; Trouble sleeping; Extreme fever; Prolonged Erection; Infection in the respiratory system; Sore Throat; Runny Nose; Bronchitis; Nausea; Vomiting.

Drug interaction

If you are taking nitrates along with this medicine, you should avoid them altogether. You should not take Anaconda If you are taking poppers. If you take any antifungal drugs.

Missed dose

In case of a missed dose, you should take it while keeping in mind that the medicine is going to show its effect after an hour of consuming it. Do not taking more than one pill in a frame of 24 hours.

Overdose

Taking an overdose of Anaconda tablets can have its own sets of side effects and one should take very good care of not taking more than one pill in a frame of 24 hours.

Storage

Anaconda needs to store at room temperature away from light. It should be stored in a drug place. Keep away from children and pets.
