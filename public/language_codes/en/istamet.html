Common use

Istamet tablet is a combination antidiabetic medicine used to control blood sugar levels in adults with type 2 diabetes. It brings together Sitagliptin, a DPP-4 inhibitor, and Metformin, a biguanide. Together, they increase the levels of natural substances that lower blood sugar and help the body use insulin more effectively.

Dosage and direction

Swallow the tablet whole, and do not crush or chew it. It should be taken along with meals. To ensure optimal effect, take the medicine at the same time each day. Never exceed the recommended dose without consulting your doctor. Do not discontinue this medicine abruptly; always speak to your doctor first.

Precautions

If you have liver problems, discuss them with your doctor before starting Istamet. This medicine may affect liver function and needs to be used with caution.
Caution is advised before using Istamet if you have a history of any kidney problems. It is a good idea to consult your doctor before taking this medicine.

Contraindications

Istamet is contraindicated in patients with allergy to metformin, sitagliptin, severe renal or hepatic dysfunction, heart problems, and infection.
Istamet is not recommended in pregnant and breastfeeding women.

Possible side effect

Istamet may have common side effects such as diarrhea, upper respiratory tract infections (like a runny nose and sore throat), nausea, nasopharyngitis (common cold), headache, and abdominal discomfort.

Drug interaction

These medicines, such as amiloride and digoxin, can affect kidney function, which may increase the effects of Istamet. Steroids like prednisone and dexamethasone can raise blood sugar levels, potentially reducing the effectiveness of Istamet tablet. Some diuretics like hydrochlorothiazide can increase blood sugar, thereby decreasing the benefit of Istamet.

Missed dose

If you forget to take your dose of Istamet, take it as soon as you remember. However, if it's nearly time for your next dose, skip the missed one and continue with your regular schedule. It's important not to increase your dosage without consulting your doctor.

Overdose

Overdosing on the Istamet could lead to common side effects becoming more pronounced. If you suspect an overdose, consult your doctor for guidance. Avoid taking more medicine than prescribed.

Storage

Store Istamet tablet at room temperature, ideally between 20°C to 25°C. Protect this medicine from light and moisture. Keep it out of the sight and reach of children and pets.
