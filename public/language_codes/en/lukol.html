Common use

Lukol is used for addressing leukorrhea (abnormal vaginal discharge) and pelvic inflammatory disease (PID) by combating bacteria and fungi responsible for these conditions. This tablet contains Spreading Hogweed (Punarnava) which provides anti-inflammatory and pain-relieving benefits, reducing discomfort associated with gynecological issues. The formulation helps maintain the normal composition of vaginal flora, supporting overall vaginal health and managing infections. Lukol improves blood circulation and strengthens uterine musculature, supporting the overall health and function of the uterus. This tablet helps support the regularity of the menstrual cycle, supporting the management of menstrual ailments.

Dosage and direction

This tablet must be taken as directed by the healthcare provider.

Precautions

Pregnant or lactating women and people with any medical condition should consult a doctor before use.
Stay hydrated for better absorption of nutrients. Avoid consumption of alcohol while on medication.

Contraindications

Contains natural ingredients. However, if you have any sensitivities to its ingredients, avoid using it. In case of an allergic reaction, stop using the product and consult a healthcare professional.

Possible side effect

It is generally safe to use as indicated. In case you have an allergic reaction, please discontinue the use and consult your doctor immediately.

Drug interaction

Lukol tablets can often be used with other supplements or medications. However, it is always advisable to contact a healthcare expert to verify that there are no interactions.

Missed dose

If you miss a dose of Lukol, take it as soon as possible. However, if it is almost time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and go back to your regular schedule. Do not double the dose.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical attention.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry place. Keep away from direct sunlight and strong-smelling substances.
