Common use

Reosto is used for supporting and maintaining bone health through a blend of natural ingredients. Arjuna, Indian Bdellium, and Bala contribute to strengthening bones and promoting healthy bone metabolism. It helps alleviate the pain and inflammation and manages prolonged immobilization following multiple or complex fractures or surgery for multiple fractures.

Dosage and direction

1-2 tablets twice daily. Refer to the product label for detailed instructions on dosage and usage.

Precautions

Consult a healthcare professional before use, particularly if you are pregnant, nursing, or on other medications.
Do not exceed the recommended dosage.
This product is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.

Contraindications

Free from common allergens. In case of an allergic reaction, please consult a healthcare provider and stop using it immediately.
Reosto tablet is formulated for adults. Consult a healthcare professional before giving this supplement to children.

Possible side effect

The product is generally safe when used as directed. For any adverse reactions or unusual symptoms, discontinue use and consult a healthcare professional.

Drug interaction

It is advisable to consult a healthcare professional before combining Reosto tablet with other supplements or medications to avoid potential interactions.

Missed dose

Take the medicine as soon as you can, but skip the missed dose if it is almost time for your next dose. Do not take two doses at one time.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical attention or call your doctor.

Storage

Store in a cool and dry place, away from heat and sunlight. Do not refrigerate. Keep out of reach of children.
