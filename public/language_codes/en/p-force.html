Common use

P-Force capsules contain Sildenafil Citrate, a potent medication for treating erectile dysfunction. Enhancing blood flow to the penis it facilitates firm erections, restoring confidence and improving sexual performance.

Dosage and direction

P-Force capsules should generally be taken half an hour to an hour before scheduled sexual activity. Avoiding grapefruit juice and grapefruits is advised, as is alcohol. P-Force is taken orally, which means it is swallowed with water. Avoid taking more than one tablet per day. Use as needed, not for daily consumption.

Precautions

To prevent potential side effects, it is advised to restrict alcohol intake when using P-Force capsules. You must consult a doctor before starting the P-Force capsule, particularly if you have concerns about your liver’s condition. If you have kidney issues, seeking advice from a medical professional before initiating treatment with P-Force capsule is advisable.

Contraindications

P-Force is contraindicated in patients with a history of hypersensitivity to Sildenafil. It is also contraindicated in patients with pulmonary veno-occlusive disease, stomach ulcer, heart disease, kidney disease, liver disease, patients who had a heart attack, heart failure, angina, irregular heartbeat, stroke, chest pain, high or low blood pressure, high cholesterol, bleeding disorder, blood circulation problems, blood cell problems such as sickle cell anemia, multiple myeloma, leukemia, eye disease or diabetes.

Possible side effect

Common Side Effects: Headache; Flushing; Changes in vision; Sudden loss of hearing; Upset stomach; Nasal congestion; Persistent erection (priapism).

Drug interaction

Avoid taking nitrates (isordil) with P-Force. It may affect the safety and effectiveness of P-Force.

Missed dose

If you miss a dose of P-Force, take it as soon as you remember. But, if the next dose is near, skip the missed dose and resume the regular dosing regime.

Overdose

In case you accidentally take an overdose of this medication and experience severe side effects or worsening of any of the symptoms, please consult your doctor. Strict adherence to the prescribed dose is essential.

Storage

Store P-Force in a cool, dry place below 30°C. Protect the medicine from direct sunlight and moisture to maintain its effectiveness. Keep the product out of reach and sight of children to prevent accidental ingestion.
