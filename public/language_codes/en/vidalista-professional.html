Common use

Vidalista Professional is a medicine used in Erectile Dysfunction treatment. Vidalista Professional contains Tadalafil which is used to provide relief from Erectile Dysfunction (ED) by increasing the flow of blood to the penis. It is an Impotence medication that works by inhibiting the PDE5 enzyme. It can also be recommended to treat Pulmonary Arterial Hypertension (PAH) and Benign Prostatic Hypertrophy (enlarged prostate) by your doctor.

Dosage and direction

Vidalista Professional tablets should be consumed as per the instructions provided by your healthcare provider.
The general guidelines for taking Vidalista Professional are:
Take about 30-60 minutes before planned sexual activity.
Swallow the tablet with water or juice; do not crush, chew, or break it.
It can be taken with or without food, but taking it on an empty stomach may help it work faster.
Do not take more than one tablet within a 24-hour period.

Precautions

With any drug, especially if you plan on using it for the first time, you need to ensure that you are well aware of all the precautions associated with taking that drug. Here are the precautions you need to know, if you want to take this medication:
If you suffer from a severe liver disease and you want to take this drug, consult your doctor first.
If you have taken this drug, do not drive as it can bring about sleepiness, tiredness, dizziness and it can also drop your level of awareness.
If you suffer from any kidney disease, before you use Vidalista Professional, talk to your doctor.
If you take this drug, you cannot consume alcohol as the drug drops the blood pressure level and alcohol combined with the drug, drops the blood pressure even lower.

Contraindications

People with liver disease may require constant observation of their liver functions. Doctors usually prescribe lower strengths of Vidalista Professional to them.
People with kidney problems need to be monitored to prevent further kidney damage. They are usually recommended low-strength doses in the beginning.
Vidalista Professional is not advised for women, and its effects on pregnant or breastfeeding women have not been studied extensively.
Avoid grapefruit juice and alcohol consumption while taking Vidalista Professional, as they can have adverse effects.

Possible side effect

Vidalista Professional may cause some mild side effects that can resolve on their own in some time. Vidalista Professional side effects include: Flushing; Headache; Dizziness; Stuffy nose; Muscle pain.
These symptoms usually go away on their own. However, you are advised to contact your doctor if any of these symptoms worsen.

Drug interaction

Avoid taking Vidalista Professional with medications such as Erythrityl Tetranitrate, Isosorbide Dinitrate, Isosorbide Mononitrate, Nitroglycerin, Pentaerythritol Tetranitrate.
Toxicity of Vidalista Professional are increased by erythromycin, ketoconazole (Nizoral), itraconazole (Sporanox), indinavir (Crixivan) and ritonavir (Norvir).
Inform your doctor if you are taking Atazanavir, Bunazosin, Clarithromycin, Doxazosin, Erythromycin, Indinavir, Itraconazole, Ketoconazole, Moxisylyte, Nefazodone, Nelfinavir, Phenoxybenzamine, Phentolamine, Prazosin, Saquinavir, Telithromycin, Terazosin, Trimazosin, Urapidil as your Vidalista Professional dose may require correction.

Missed dose

If you miss a dose of Vidalista Professional, take it as soon as you remember. But, if the next dose is near, skip the missed dose and resume the regular dosing regime.

Overdose

Overdose causes symptoms described as side effects. Seek for immediate medical help if you by your opinion you took too much of this medication.

Storage

Store at room temperature away from moisture, sunlight, kids and pets in a tight container.
