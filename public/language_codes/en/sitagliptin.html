Common use

Sitagliptin is used with diet and exercise to improve blood sugar control in adults with type 2 diabetes mellitus (not for type 1 diabetes).

Dosage and direction

Use the medicine exactly as directed. The specific dosage is determined by a doctor based on individual needs and kidney function. Take it at the same time every day to maintain a consistent routine. Swallow the tablet whole; do not break, chew, or crush it. You can take it with or without food.

Precautions

Tell your doctor if you have or have ever had: heart problems such as heart failure; pancreatitis; high triglycerides (a type of fat in the blood); gallstones; alcoholism; or kidney disease (or if you are on dialysis).
It is not known if sitagliptin will harm an unborn baby. Follow your doctor's instructions about using sitagliptin if you are pregnant or you become pregnant. Controlling diabetes is very important during pregnancy. Ask a doctor if it is safe to breastfeed while using this medicine.

Contraindications

You should not use sitagliptin if you are allergic to it or any of its ingredients.
Call your doctor if you have symptoms of heart failure: shortness of breath (even while lying down), swelling in your legs or feet, rapid weight gain.
Stop taking sitagliptin and call your doctor if you have symptoms of pancreatitis: severe pain in your upper stomach spreading to your back, with or without vomiting.

Possible side effect

Common side effects of sitagliptin may include: low blood sugar (if you also use insulin or another oral diabetes medication); cold symptoms such as stuffy or runny nose, sneezing, sore throat; or headache.

Drug interaction

When you start or stop taking sitagliptin, your doctor may need to adjust the doses of other medicines you use on a regular basis. Tell your doctor about all your other medicines, especially: insulin or oral diabetes medicine.

Missed dose

Take the medicine as soon as you can, but skip the missed dose if it is almost time for your next dose. Do not take two doses at one time.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical attention.

Storage

Store the tablets at room temperature away from moisture and heat. Keep away from children and pets.
