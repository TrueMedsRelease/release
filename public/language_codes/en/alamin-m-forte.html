Common use

Alamin M Forte Capsule is a dietary supplement enriched with essential amino acids and key minerals, including L-Isoleucine, L-Leucine, L-Lysine, copper, manganese, and selenium. It supports protein synthesis, cellular repair, and metabolic balance, contributing to overall health, supports muscle growth and recovery, boosts immune function, improves nervous system health, provides antioxidant protection.

Dosage and direction

Take one capsule twice daily with water, preferably after a meal. Do not chew or crush the capsule, swallow it as a whole with water. Do not exceed the recommended dosage.

Precautions

Pregnant and lactating women and people with any medical condition should consult a doctor before consuming this product. Inform your doctor if you have existing liver or kidney diseases.

Contraindications

Do not take if you have a history of allergic reactions to any ingredient in the formulation. Do not exceed the recommended daily dose to avoid adverse effects.

Possible side effect

While generally well-tolerated, potential side effects include nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, abdominal pain, loss of appetite, headache, and skin rashes. If severe, seek medical attention immediately.

Drug interaction

Alamin M Forte capsules may interact with medications like aspirin, barbiturates, cimetidine, ciprofloxacin, chloramphenicol, phenytoin, cisplatin, altretamine, and methotrexate. It is advised to avoid combining with activated charcoal or calcium gluconate. Consult a doctor before taking other supplements to avoid adverse effects.

Missed dose

Take the medicine as soon as you can, but skip the missed dose if it is almost time for your next dose. Do not take two doses at one time.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical attention.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry and dark place. Keep away from direct sunlight, heat and moisture. Keep out of the reach of children and pets.
