Common use

Cenforce Professional is an effective medication that boosts blood flow to the penis and helps men gain a firm erection and functions effectively as an Erectile Dysfunction (ED) or Impotence treatment. Its key ingredient is Sildenafil Citrate, which belongs to a class of drugs known as Phosphodiesterase (PDE-5) inhibitors.

Dosage and direction

Here are certain general guidelines you should follow on how to take Cenforce Professional: Take Cenforce Professional at least 30-60 minutes before engaging in planned sexual activity; Do not crush, break, or chew the tablet, but swallow the tablet as a whole; You can take this ED pill on an empty stomach or after a light meal; Do not take more than one dose of Cenforce Professional in 24 hours.

Precautions

Doctors usually monitor liver functions regularly if a person is using Sildenafil with severe liver diseases. It is best to exercise extra caution in such cases.
It is best to consult your healthcare provider before taking Cenforce Professional if you have any kind of kidney impairments.
It is best to avoid drinking grapefruit juice or alcohol with Sildenafil tablets.

Contraindications

Cenforce Professional is contraindicated in patients with a history of hypersensitivity to Sildenafil. It is also contraindicated in patients with pulmonary veno-occlusive disease, stomach ulcer, heart disease, kidney disease, liver disease, patients who had a heart attack, heart failure, angina, irregular heartbeat, stroke, chest pain, high or low blood pressure, high cholesterol, bleeding disorder, blood circulation problems, blood cell problems such as sickle cell anemia, multiple myeloma, leukemia, eye disease or diabetes.

Possible side effect

Side effects of Cenforce Professional: Headache; Heartburn; Diarrhea; Nausea; Flushing; Nosebleeds; Pain in muscles, arms, and legs.

Drug interaction

Avoid taking nitrates (isordil) with Cenforce Professional. It may affect the safety and effectiveness of Sildenafil.

Missed dose

If you miss a dose of Cenforce Professional, it is usually not a serious issue. Take it before your sexual activity when needed, and avoid taking double dosage in a day.

Overdose

Overdosing on Cenforce Professional can cause certain side effects like dizziness and prolonged and painful erection. If you overdose on this tablet, it is best to consult your doctor immediately.

Storage

Store Cenforce Professional tablets in an airtight container in a cool, dry place. Keep them out of the reach of children.
