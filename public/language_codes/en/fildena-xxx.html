Common use

Fildena XXX is a fruit-flavored chewable medicine formulated to help treat Erectile Dysfunction in men. The medicine improves blood supply to the penis, thus making it easier to attain and maintain an erection during sexual activity. Fildena XXX is contain Sildenafil Citrate.

Dosage and direction

Fildena XXX should always be consumed as per instructions provided by your healthcare provider. Fildena XXX has fast action within 30-60 minutes, you can take pill about 30 minutes before indulging in sexual activity; Convenient fruity flavor chewable tablet; Convenient for on-the-go use without the need for water; Do not take more than one tablet of Fildena XXX within a 24-hour period.

Precautions

Since Fildena XXX is not recommended for women, there is limited data on its safety during pregnancy and breastfeeding women. It's essential to avoid using medications not prescribed by a healthcare provider during pregnancy.
Avoid or limit consumption of alcohol and grapefruit juice while taking Fildena XXX, as they may cause side effects like dizziness or prolonged erection.
Caution is advised if you have to undergo surgery, or dental treatment.

Contraindications

Fildena XXX is contraindicated in patients with a history of hypersensitivity to Sildenafil. It is also contraindicated in patients with pulmonary veno-occlusive disease, stomach ulcer, heart disease, kidney disease, liver disease, patients who had a heart attack, heart failure, angina, irregular heartbeat, stroke, chest pain, high or low blood pressure, high cholesterol, bleeding disorder, blood circulation problems, blood cell problems such as sickle cell anemia, multiple myeloma, leukemia, eye disease or diabetes.

Possible side effect

Some of these Fildena XXX side effects include: Flushing; Headache; Dizziness; Stuffy nose; Ringing or buzzing in ears; Muscle pain.

Drug interaction

Avoid taking nitrates (isordil) with Fildena XXX. It may affect the safety and effectiveness of Sildenafil.

Missed dose

If you miss a dose of Fildena XXX, take it as soon as you remember. But, if the next dose is near, skip the missed dose and resume the regular dosing regime.

Overdose

In case you accidentally take an overdose of Fildena XXX and experience severe side effects or worsening of any of the symptoms, please consult your doctor. Strict adherence to the prescribed dose is essential.

Storage

Store Fildena XXX at room temperature (20°C to 25°C), away from direct sunlight, heat, and moisture. Keep it out of reach of children.
