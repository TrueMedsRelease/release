Common use

Neurobion Forte Injection is used to treat vitamin B complex deficiencies and support nerve function. It effectively alleviates symptoms of peripheral neuropathy such as numbness, tingling, and muscle weakness, aiding in nerve repair and overall neurological health. In addition to addressing vitamin B deficiencies, this medicine is used to manage several health conditions like pernicious anaemia, peripheral neuropathy related to vitamin B12 deficiency, alcoholism or malnutrition, and metabolic disorders like homocystinuria.

Dosage and direction

Administer Neurobion Forte Injection as follows: The medicine is administered through deep intramuscular injection, usually in the gluteal region. Ensure the injection is only administered by a qualified professional. Intravenous administration is not advised due to potential adverse effects.

Precautions

Due to its role in fetal neural development, Neurobion Forte Injection is often used as an adjunct supplement in pregnancy. However, it's best to consult your doctor before using this medicine during pregnancy.
While B vitamins are generally considered safe during breastfeeding, you should still discuss with your doctor before using Neurobion Forte Injection if you are lactating.
If you have liver health issues, consult your doctor before using this medicine, as the liver plays a key role in metabolising the ingredients in the injection.
Neurobion Forte Injection should be used with care if you have kidney problems. Consult your doctor before using this medicine if you have a history of kidney disorders.

Contraindications

If you know that you are allergic to any components of Neurobion Forte Injection, do not use this product.
Parkinson’s (Levodopa): Pyridoxine may reduce levodopa effect. Polycythemia Vera: May trigger symptoms early after B12 injection.

Possible side effect

Neurobion Forte Injection can cause the following side effects: Pain and redness at the injection site; Itching; Headache; Flushing.

Drug interaction

Anticoagulants or antiplatelet medications: Neurobion Forte Injection can increase the risk of bleeding when used with anticoagulants like warfarin or antiplatelet medications such as clopidogrel.
Anti-Parkinsonism medications: Neurobion Forte Injection may reduce the effectiveness of levodopa, a medication for Parkinson's disease.

Missed dose

If you've missed a dose of Neurobion Forte Injection, administer it as soon as you remember. If it's nearly time for your next dose, skip the missed one and continue with your regular schedule. Don't administer extra doses to make up for a missed one.

Overdose

An overdose of Neurobion Forte Injection might cause symptoms such as itching, headache, or flushing. If you've administered more than the prescribed dose, inform your doctor promptly.

Storage

Keep Neurobion Forte Injection at or below 25°C, ideally in a cool, dry place. Shield it from direct light and moisture. Store it in a place out of children's reach.
