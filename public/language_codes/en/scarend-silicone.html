Common use

Scarend Silicone gel is designed to improve the appearance of scars, including acne marks, surgical scars, and stretch marks. Its advanced silicone-based formula penetrates deeply to soften scar tissue, enhance healing, and promote smoother, more even-toned skin. It can improve skin texture, reduce visibility of scars, and support long-term skin recovery.

Dosage and direction

Wash the scar area and pat dry. Apply a very thin coat of Scarend Silicone gel directly to the affected area/scar area. To maintain coverage, apply twice daily or as directed by the healthcare provider.

Precautions

Do not use it on open wounds. Avoid contact with eyes, mucous membrane and third degree burns; in case of accidental contact, rinse with plenty of water.
Consult a dermatologist if irritation, redness, or discomfort occurs after application. Perform a patch test before first use to check for any allergic reactions or irritation. Avoid using other strong skincare medications to reduce irritation.

Contraindications

Does not contain any common allergens. However, if you have any sensitivities to its ingredients, avoid using it. If you experience any allergic reactions after using the product, discontinue if redness or irritation occurs.

Possible side effect

Scarend Silicone gel is generally safe and well-tolerated, but it may cause mild side effects such as skin irritation, redness, itching, burning, or discomfort at the application site.

Drug interaction

Scarend Silicone gel as it is a topical gel, it should not be applied concurrently with other topical medicines (like antibacterials or corticosteroids) on the same area.

Missed dose

If you miss a dose of Scarend Silicone gel, you should apply it as soon as you remember. However, if it is almost time for your next scheduled application, skip the missed dose and continue with your regular schedule.

Overdose

Overdose of Scarend Silicone gel is unlikely as it is intended for external use only. Using the prescribed dosage is recommended to avoid any side effects.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry and dark place, temperature below 30 degree celsius. Keep away from direct sunlight and moisture. Do not freeze. Keep out of reach of children and pets.
