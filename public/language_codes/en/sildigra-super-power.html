Common use

Sildigra Super Power is a combination medication containing Sildenafil Citrate and Dapoxetine Hydrochloride. It is used to treat Erectile Dysfunction (ED) and Premature Ejaculation (PE) in adult men. Sildenafil increases blood flow to the penis by stopping the PDE-5 enzyme and increasing cyclic Guanosine Monophosphate (cGMP) levels, helping men achieve a firm erection when sexually stimulated. Dapoxetine inhibits contraction responses during sexual activities, preventing premature ejaculation. Together, these actions address both ED and PE.

Dosage and direction

Take Sildigra Super Power tablet 1-3 hours before planned sexual activity. It is used as needed, so there is no regular dosing schedule. Swallow the tablet whole with water; do not break, crush, or split it. For quicker action, taking Sildigra Super Power on an empty stomach or after a light meal is advisable. Avoid heavy meals and grapefruit juice. Limit alcohol consumption to prevent side effects. Do not take more than one dose within 24 hours.

Precautions

Sildigra Super Power should be used cautiously in patients with liver conditions. Consult your doctor for advice personalized to your health.
If you have kidney issues, discuss them with your doctor before using Sildigra Super Power to avoid potential complications.
Sildigra Super Power is not intended for women. Pregnant and breastfeeding women should not consume it.

Contraindications

Sildigra Super Power is contraindicated in patients with a history of hypersensitivity to Sildenafil or Dapoxetine. It is also contraindicated in patients with pulmonary veno-occlusive disease, stomach ulcer, heart disease, kidney disease, liver disease, patients who had a heart attack, heart failure, angina, irregular heartbeat, stroke, chest pain, high or low blood pressure, high cholesterol, bleeding disorder, blood circulation problems, blood cell problems such as sickle cell anemia, multiple myeloma, leukemia, eye disease or diabetes. Also epilepsy, mental health problems like bipolar disorder, depression, or mania.

Possible side effect

Side effects of Sildigra Super Power: Headache; Heartburn; Diarrhea; Nausea; Flushing; Nosebleeds; Feeling dizzy; Lightheaded; Feeling sweaty; Feeling confused; Pain in muscles, arms, and legs.

Drug interaction

Avoid taking nitrates (isordil) with Sildenafil. It may affect the safety and effectiveness of Sildenafil.
Avoid taking antipsychotic medications (benzodiazepines), anticoagulant medications (acenocoumarol), cancer medications (abemaciclib), antidiabetic medications (acarbose), antihypertensives (beta-blockers such as acebutolol), and non-steroidal anti-inflammatory drugs (aceclofenac). These medications may alter the safety and effectiveness of this medication.

Missed dose

You are unlikely to miss a dose of this medicine as it is to be taken only when needed. Do not take more than one dose within 24 hours.

Overdose

In case of accidental overdose, consult your doctor if you experience severe side effects or worsening of any of the symptoms. Strict adherence to the prescribed dose is essential.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and moisture. Keep out of reach of children and pets.
