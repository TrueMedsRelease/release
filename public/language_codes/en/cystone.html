Common use

Cystone tablet is used to support urinary tract health by helping manage urinary stones and infections. It promotes overall urinary system wellness with its natural ingredients.

Dosage and direction

Generally, the dosage begins with two tablets twice daily with meals and water, followed by one tablet twice daily or as directed by your physician.

Precautions

Pregnant and lactating women should consult the doctor before taking this medicine.
Do not exceed the recommended dosage.
Restrict eating tobacco or drinking alcohol while taking this medicine.
Avoid strong smells in the mouth like onion, mint, coffee, camphor, or garlic while taking this medicine.

Contraindications

Not recommended for children under 14 years of age. Food supplements should not be used as a substitute for a balanced and varied diet.
Contains natural ingredients. However, if you have any sensitivities to its ingredients, avoid using it. In case of an allergic reaction, stop using the product and consult a healthcare professional.

Possible side effect

Cystone tablet is generally safe to use as indicated. In case you have an allergic reaction, please discontinue the use and consult your doctor immediately.

Drug interaction

Other drugs may interact with Cystone, including prescription and over-the-counter medicines, vitamins, and herbal products. Tell each of your health care providers about all medicines you use now and any medicine you start or stop using.

Missed dose

If you miss a dose of Cystone, take it as soon as possible. However, if it is almost time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and go back to your regular schedule. Do not double the dose.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical attention.

Storage

Store in a cool dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep out of reach of the children.
