Common use

Cenforce-D is used for Premature Ejaculation Treatment and Erectile Dysfunction. As active ingredients, these tablets contain 100 mg of Sildenafil Citrate and 60 mg of Dapoxetine.
Cenforce-D, which is a combination of two drugs, helps you in the treatment of Premature Ejaculation and Erectile Dysfunction. In the first condition, it occurs when men cannot delay ejaculation. It may be due to multiple reasons that are both biological and psychological. While in the second condition, men cannot keep their erection firm while making love to their partners.

Dosage and direction

The doses of Cenforce-D vary as per the requirement of patients. Hence, you need to consult your doctor for the correct dosage for your condition.
Therefore, some points need to be kept in mind while taking this medication:
You can take Cenforce-D with a glass of water from 30 minutes to 1 hour prior to your sexual activity.
The passable time for the medicine to take action is within one to three hours of intake.
When taking medication, avoid intake of high-fat meals. It may affect the action of the drug.

Precautions

It's better to avoid the consumption of alcohol while taking the pills.
It will also help if you avoid the consumption of grapefruit juice as it may interact with medicine and decrease its effectiveness.
It is also advised to avoid driving and operating heavy machinery while taking medication.

Contraindications

If you have any problems listed below you must not be treated with Cenforce-D pills: heart or cardiovascular diseases, strokes, heart attacks, angina, blood pressure problems, retinitis pigmentosa, anaemia and HIV problems are regarded as main contraindications. It is important to consult a doctor to find out all personal peculiarities of treatment.

Possible side effect

If you experience some of these symptoms you must visit a doctor immediately: Headache; Sleeplessness; Dizziness; Painful urination; Prolonged and painful erection; Burning, tingling, numbness in the arms and feet; Indigestion.

Drug interaction

Cenforce-D components interact with a number of other chemical substances. You should not take this medication with the special drugs listed below: Nitroprusside drugs; Amylnitrate; Anti-depressants; Serotonin elevating medicines; Tramadol; Linezolid. Note that about 7 days must pass after Cenforce-D therapy before you can take these drugs.

Missed dose

When missing a dose stick to your usual scheme of treatment and simply skip a missed portion. Never take a doubled dose because it can lead to overdose. Remember that the safe recommended dosage presupposes taking only one tablet per day.

Overdose

Severe symptoms of overdose can be dangerous for your life. That is why you must refer to a professional medical service if you suspect overdose. The influences of large portions can be characterized by severe side effects which last for a long time.

Storage

Keep Cenforce-D at room temperature, away from heat, moisture, and direct light. It's also better to check the expiry dates before taking medicine, and don't take it if it has crossed the date.
