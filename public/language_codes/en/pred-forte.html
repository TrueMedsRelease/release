Common use

Pred Forte Ophthalmic Suspension is a prescription medication for various eye conditions. Which contains the active ingredient prednisolone acetate, which belongs to the class of corticosteroids. This medication is specifically formulated for ophthalmic use and administered directly to the eye.
This medicine is commonly prescribed to reduce inflammation and redness in the eye. It works by suppressing the immune response and reducing the release of inflammatory substances in the eye.

Dosage and direction

Pred Forte Ophthalmic Suspension is intended for external use only. It should be used according to your doctor's prescribed dose and duration.
To administer the eye drops, hold the dropper near the eye without touching it.
Gently squeeze the dropper to release the medicine into the lower eyelid.
Any excess liquid can be wiped off. It is essential to use the medication correctly to ensure its effectiveness and avoid potential complications.

Precautions

Consult your doctor before using Pred Forte Ophthalmic Suspension if you are pregnant or planning to become pregnant, or if breastfeeding. They will weigh the potential benefits against possible risks and recommend the appropriate action.
If you have a liver condition, it is essential to inform your doctor before using Pred Forte Ophthalmic Suspension. They will evaluate your liver function and determine if any dosage adjustments or alternative treatments are necessary.
Pred Forte Ophthalmic Suspension may temporarily cause blurred vision or other visual disturbances. It is recommended to avoid driving or operating machinery until your vision clears up and returns to normal.

Contraindications

Avoid using it if you are allergic to any of the ingredients in Pred Forte Ophthalmic Suspension. You must inform your doctor about known allergies or hypersensitivity reactions to medications.
Pred Forte Ophthalmic Suspension is not recommended for children under 12. Older patients may be more susceptible to specific side effects of Pred Forte Ophthalmic Suspension. Close monitoring by a doctor is essential to ensure safety and efficacy.
Don't use Pred Forte if you have next conditions/diseases: Bacterial, viral or fungal eye infections; Ulcers or injuries on the cornea; Glaucoma; Tuberculosis of the eye, or herpes simplex.

Possible side effect

Typical side effects of Pred Forte Ophthalmic Suspension include:
Headaches: The suspension may cause a headache as a side effect in some individuals.
Burning sensation: Some people may experience a burning sensation while using Pred Forte Ophthalmic Suspension.
Eye irritation: Pred Forte Ophthalmic Suspension may cause eye irritation in some individuals.
Watery eyes: Some individuals may experience watery eyes while using Pred Forte Ophthalmic Suspension.
Blurred vision: This medication can sometimes lead to blurred vision.

Drug interaction

Pred Forte may interact with the following drugs: Cobicistat and Ritonavir.

Missed dose

If you forget a dose of Pred Forte, take it as soon as you remember. However, if it is almost time for your next scheduled dose, it is better to skip the missed dose and continue with your regular dosing schedule.

Overdose

Exceeding the recommended dosage can lead to headaches, burning sensations, irritation, and watery eyes. If you suspect an overdose, it is crucial to seek immediate medical help to prevent any potential harm.
