Common use

Acetyl-L-Carnitine capsules is used for promoting cellular energy production and cognitive performance. This supplement contains Acetyl L-Carnitine, a compound known for its role in transporting fatty acids to produce energy. Ideal for those seeking enhanced mental clarity, focus, and physical endurance.

Dosage and direction

Take one capsule daily with water after meals or as directed by a dietitian. Do not chew or crush the capsule, swallow it as a whole with water.

Precautions

Pregnant and lactating women and people with any medical condition should consult a dietitian before consuming Acetyl-L-Carnitine. The product is not to be used as a substitute for a varied diet.

Contraindications

Acetyl-L-Carnitine should be avoided or used with caution if you have seizure disorders, hypothyroidism, bipolar disorder, or are taking blood thinners like warfarin.

Possible side effect

Acetyl-L-Carnitine is generally safe, but common side effects include nausea, vomiting, stomach cramps, diarrhea, headaches, restlessness, and a distinct "fishy" body odor.

Drug interaction

Consult your physician before use if you are on any medications to ensure safety.

Missed dose

If you miss a dose of Acetyl-L-Carnitine, take it as soon as you remember. However, if it is almost time for your next scheduled dose, skip the missed dose and return to your regular dosing schedule.

Overdose

Acetyl-L-Carnitine intake at high levels, may result in gastrointestinal distress, including nausea, vomiting, abdominal cramps, and diarrhea.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry and dark place. Keep away from direct sunlight, heat and moisture. Keep out of the reach of children and pets.
