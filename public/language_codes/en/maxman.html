Common use

Maxman tablet is an Ayurvedic medicine that can help to maximize strength and stamina. It combines 13 completely natural herbs. All the herbs contained in the Maxman tablets are known to increase power, stamina, vigor, vitality, and timing by reducing stress, fatigue, and physical weakness. Maxman tablets helps to boost testosterone levels and maintains sexual strength and stamina.

Dosage and direction

Take one tablet twice a day after a meal with lukewarm milk or water. Take it for at least 3-4 months regularly to improve strength, stamina, energy, and bring excitement in life. You can stop consuming once you feel changes in your performance.

Precautions

Consult with a healthcare professional before starting any new dietary supplement, including Maxman Ayurvedic capsules.
Maxman capsules are not intended to replace medical procedures or treatments.

Contraindications

You should not use Maxman if you are allergic to any ingredient of the product, including all herbs in combines: Shilajit, Ashwagandha, Shatavari, Gokshura, Safed Musli, Dalchini, Akarkara, and Kesar.

Possible side effect

There are no known side effects, but if you experience any discomfort call your doctor for medical advice.

Drug interaction

Other drugs may affect Maxman, including prescription and over-the-counter medicines, vitamins, and herbal products. Tell your doctor about all other medicines you use.

Missed dose

Take the medicine as soon as you can, but skip the missed dose if it is almost time for your next dose. Do not take two doses at one time.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical attention.

Storage

Store at room temperature away from moisture and heat. Keep out of reach of the children.
