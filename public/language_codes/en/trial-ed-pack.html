Dosage and direction

Take Viagra, Cialis and Levitra exactly as it was prescribed for you. Viagra, Cialis and Levitra is usually taken only when needed, 30 minutes to 1 hour before sexual activity. You may take it up to 4 hours before sexual activity. Do not take Viagra, Cialis and Levitra more than once per day.

Precautions

To make sure that Viagra, Cialis or Levitra are safe for you, tell your doctor if you have ever had: heart disease or heart rhythm problems; a heart attack or stroke; angina (chest pain), high or low blood pressure; pulmonary hypertension (unless you are taking tadalafil for this); a condition called pulmonary veno-occlusive disease (narrowing of the veins that carry blood from the lungs to the heart); liver or kidney disease (or if you are on dialysis); vision loss, or retinitis pigmentosa (an inherited condition of the eye); a bleeding disorder; a blood cell disorder such as sickle cell anemia, multiple myeloma, or leukemia; a physical deformity of the penis (such as Peyronie's disease), or an erection lasting longer than 4 hours; a stomach ulcer; or health problems that make sexual activity unsafe.

Contraindications

You should not take Viagra, Cialis or Levitra if you are allergic to Sildenafil, Tadalafil or Vardenafil.
Taking Viagra, Cialis or Levitra with certain other medicines can cause a sudden and serious decrease in blood pressure. Do not take Viagra, Cialis and Levitra if you also use: riociguat (to treat pulmonary arterial hypertension); or a nitrate drug (for chest pain or heart problems) such as nitroglycerin, isosorbide dinitrate, isosorbide mononitrate, or recreational drugs such as amyl nitrate or nitrite "poppers".

Possible side effect

Common side effects may include: headache; flushing (warmth, redness, or tingly feeling); nausea, upset stomach; stuffy nose; or muscle pain, back pain, pain in your arms, legs, or back.

Drug interaction

Tell your doctor about all your current medicines and any you start or stop using, especially: medicines to treat erectile dysfunction or pulmonary arterial hypertension; an antibiotic or antifungal medicine; antiviral medicine to treat hepatitis C or HIV/AIDS; drugs to treat high blood pressure or a prostate disorder; or seizure medicine.

Missed dose

As Viagra, Cialis and Levitra is used as needed, you are not likely to be on a dosing schedule. Do not take two doses at one time.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical attention. Call your doctor or seek emergency medical attention if your erection is painful or lasts longer than 4 hours or other side effects do not go away.

Storage

Store at room temperature away from moisture and heat. Keep away from children.
