Common use

Tugain Gel 5% belongs to the vasodilator class of medicines. It is used to help men with male pattern baldness grow more hair. Tugain Gel 5% should only be applied to the scalp in the dosage and manner recommended on the package or by your doctor.

Dosage and direction

Tugain 5% Gel should only be used externally. Use it according to your doctor's instructions for dosage and duration. Before using, read the directions on the label. Before you use it, make sure your scalp is clean and dry. Hair growth can take anywhere from two to four months to notice. It is possible that the first growth will be soft, colorless, and scarcely noticeable. Using more than the suggested amount will not enhance hair growth and may raise the risk of negative effects. To maintain hair growth, this medicine must be used on a regular basis. Tell your doctor if your condition does not improve or worsens after 4 to 6 months of use.

Precautions

Individuals with heart conditions should exercise caution as Tugain Gel 5% may cause rapid heartbeat or chest pain.
Those with scalp conditions or irritations should also be cautious as this medication is applied topically to the scalp.

Contraindications

Use of Tugain Gel 5% is strictly contraindicated in pregnancy and breastfeeding. If you are pregnant or breastfeeding, consult your doctor before using this medication.
Tugain Gel 5% is contraindicated in individuals who have a known hypersensitivity to either of its two components: minoxidil or ethanol.

Possible side effect

Side effects of Tugain Gel 5%: Irritation of the scalp; Dryness of the scalp; Scaling or flaking of the scalp; Itching of the scalp; Burning sensation on the scalp.

Drug interaction

Tugain Gel 5% may interact unfavorably when combined with other topical treatments for the scalp like antifungal creams (like ketoconazole), topical corticosteroids (e.g., betamethasone) may lead to skin irritation or altered effectiveness of both products.

Missed dose

In case of a missed dose, continue with the next scheduled dose. Do not attempt to make up for the missed dose by applying the extra solution.

Overdose

An overdose of Tugain Gel 5% may result in severe side effects such as dizziness or rapid heartbeat. If you suspect an overdose, seek immediate medical attention.

Storage

Store Tugain Gel 5% in a cool and dry place. Protect it from light and moisture. Keep this solution away from children.
