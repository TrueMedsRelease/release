Common use

P-Force Fort is medication designed to treat erectile dysfunction (ED) effectively. With Sildenafil Citrate as its active ingredient, this medication helps enhance blood flow to the penis, enabling men to achieve and maintain a firm erection during sexual activity.

Dosage and direction

P-Force Fort is a prescription-based medication for Erectile Dysfunction (ED). It should always be consumed according to the instructions provided by your healthcare provider.
The general guidelines for taking P-Force Fort are: Take P-Force Fort about 60 minutes before planned sexual activity; Swallow the tablet with water; do not crush, chew, or break it; It can be taken with or without food, but taking it on an empty stomach may get you faster results; Do not take more than one tablet within a 24-hour period.

Precautions

Consultation: Always discuss your medical history with your doctor before using it.
Avoid Alcohol: Excessive alcohol consumption may reduce the effectiveness of the medication.
Drug Interactions: Inform your doctor about any ongoing medications, particularly nitrates or blood pressure drugs.
Health Conditions: Individuals with heart, liver, or kidney problems should use this medication with caution.

Contraindications

It is not recommended to take P-Force Fort for people with the following: Take Organic nitrites; Take Nitric oxide; Take Nitrates; Take Renovation to boost workout capability; Hereditary degenerative retinal disorders; Heart stroke or heart attack; Hypotension or low blood pressure; Kidney ailments; Liver damage. Consult with doctor first.

Possible side effect

Do not defer medical support in case you perceive symptoms of an allergic reaction to P-Force Fort like: Breathing difficulty; Hives; Swelling tongue, face, throat, or lips.
P-Force Fort may cause some side effects: Flushing; Headache; Dizziness; Stuffy nose; Ringing or buzzing in ears; Muscle pain in the back, arms and legs.
It's crucial follow dosage instructions carefully to minimize the risk of side effects. If you experience any side effects while taking P-Force Fort, inform your healthcare provider promptly.

Drug interaction

This Medicine augments the impact of blood pressure plummeting medicines such as: Nitroglycerin - Nitrodur; Isosorbide mononitrate - Monoket, Imdur, Ismo; Isosorbide dinitrate - Isordil; Transderm - these are mainly utilized for handling angina issues.
Not blend this Medicine with other erectile dysfunction medications like tadalafil, and vardenafil.
People ingesting the following drugs necessitate very close monitoring if they utilize P-Force fort: Mibefradil - Posicor; Atazanavir - Reyataz; Itraconazole - Sporanox; Ketoconazole - Nizoral; Erythromycin; Cimetidine - Tagamet.

Missed dose

If using as needed, a missed dose is not applicable. Do not double the dose.

Overdose

Seek immediate medical help if an overdose occurs, as it may cause severe side effects like dizziness, nausea, or prolonged erection.

Storage

Store at room temperature, below 25 C (77 F) and out of reach and children and pets.
