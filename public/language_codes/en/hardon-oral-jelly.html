Common use

HardOn Oral Jelly is a effective treatment used by men suffering from Erectile Dysfunction. This medicine works by relaxation and vasodilation (the widening of blood vessels) in the genitals. Thus, allowing for stronger and more persistent erections for the duration of satisfying sexual intercourse. HardOn Oral Jelly contains Sildenafil as its active ingredient and comes manufactured as a consumable oral jelly.

Dosage and direction

When taking HardOn Oral Jelly, it is important to remember that to experience the full therapeutic benefits of this medication, it is recommended to take it at least 10 - 15 minutes before engaging in any sexual activity. The oral jelly gets rapidly absorbed by the body and starts working quickly. Patients are advised against exceeding the recommended daily one dose (one sachet) per 24-hour period. As HardOn Oral Jelly comes as an oral jelly, the medication can be taken with or without any water and comes in a variety of wonderful flavors. This oral jelly can be taken with or without any meals, although when consuming a heavy or fatty meal can delay the onset time of this medicine.

Precautions

Caution is advised if you have to undergo surgery, or dental treatment.

Contraindications

HardOn Oral Jelly is contraindicated in patients with a history of hypersensitivity to Sildenafil. It is also contraindicated in patients with pulmonary veno-occlusive disease, stomach ulcer, heart disease, kidney disease, liver disease, patients who had a heart attack, heart failure, angina, irregular heartbeat, stroke, chest pain, high or low blood pressure, high cholesterol, bleeding disorder, blood circulation problems, blood cell problems such as sickle cell anemia, multiple myeloma, leukemia, eye disease or diabetes.

Possible side effect

Side effects of HardOn Oral Jelly: Headache; Heartburn; Diarrhea; Nausea; Flushing; Nosebleeds; Pain in muscles, arms, and legs.

Drug interaction

Avoid taking nitrates (isordil) with Sildenafil. It may affect the safety and effectiveness of Sildenafil.

Missed dose

If you miss a dose of HardOn Oral Jelly, take it as soon as you remember. But, if the next dose is near, skip the missed dose and resume the regular dosing regime. Recommended daily one dose (one sachet) per 24-hour period.

Overdose

In case you accidentally take an overdose of this medication and experience severe side effects or worsening of any of the symptoms, please consult your doctor. Strict adherence to the prescribed dose is essential.

Storage

HardOn Oral Jelly should be kept in a cool, dry place, and kept away from the reach of children.
