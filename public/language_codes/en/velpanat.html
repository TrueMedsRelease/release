Common use

Velpanat is used to manage chronic hepatitis C virus (HCV) infection in adults and children aged three years and above. It is a combination of direct-acting antivirals: sofosbuvir, a nucleotide polymerase inhibitor, and velpatasvir, an NS5A inhibitor. This medicine is effective across multiple HCV genotypes, making it suitable for patients with or without compensated cirrhosis.

Dosage and direction

Swallow the tablet whole with a glass of water. Do not crush, break, or chew the tablet. Take this medicine at the same time each day. You can take this tablet with or without food.

Precautions

The use of Velpanat during pregnancy and lactation should be done under the advice and supervision of a doctor.
Patients with renal or hepatic impairment should use Velpanat with caution, no dosage adjustments are required in these conditions, but close monitoring is advised. Patients with heart rhythm problems should use caution, especially if taking heart medicines.

Contraindications

The medicine Velpanat is contraindicated in patients who have severe hypersensitivity to either of its active components, sofosbuvir or velpatasvir.
The medicine is not recommended for patients with moderate or severe hepatic impairment (Child-Pugh B or C).

Possible side effect

Velpanat may have the following common side effects: Headache; Fatigue; Nausea; Diarrhea; Decreased appetite; Insomnia; Back pain; Anxiety.

Drug interaction

Velpanat may interact with potent P-glycoprotein (P-gp) inducers (such as rifampicin and carbamazepine), moderate to potent cytochrome P450 (CYP) inducers (like phenytoin and St. John's wort), certain anticonvulsants (phenobarbital and oxcarbazepine), and antimycobacterial (like rifabutin and rifapentine).

Missed dose

If you miss a dose, take it as soon as you remember. However, if it is close to the time of your next dose, skip the missed dose and continue with your regular schedule. Do not double dose to make up for the missed one.

Overdose

An overdose of this medication could potentially lead to severe adverse health effects. If you suspect an overdose, seek immediate medical attention.

Storage

Keep in its original container, tightly closed. Store below 30°C in a cool, dry place. Protect from excessive moisture, humidity, and direct sunlight. Store out of reach of children and pets.
