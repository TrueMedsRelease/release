Common use

Amalaki tablet is used for supporting immunity, enhancing skin health and promoting overall well-being. It is an herbal tablet formulated with Amalaki (Emblica officinalis), commonly known as Amla, a powerhouse of natural antioxidants and nutrients. Amalaki is widely known in Ayurveda for its potential. Packed with vitamin C, polyphenols and bioactive compounds, it may help the body combat oxidative stress and maintain cellular health. This supplement is designed to assist in maintaining energy levels, supporting digestion and rejuvenating the skin. It might help delay signs of premature aging, keeping the skin looking fresh and healthy. Amalaki is also known to support the body's stress response, making it beneficial for overall resilience. Regular consumption, as directed, may help build the body's natural defense, promoting wellness from within.

Dosage and direction

Take the capsule whole by mouth with a glass of water, preferably before a meal or exercise. While swallowing, you should not crush, break, or chew the pill. It is not recommended to use other drinks to administer this medicine such as grapefruit juice or alcohol because your risk of side effects may increase. Usually, the recommended dose per day is 1-2 capsules or as directed by your healthcare provider. Furthermore, you should not use this supplement more often than is prescribed by your doctor because you may experience unwanted results.

Precautions

Consult a doctor if you are pregnant or want to become pregnant before using this product. Otherwise, it may harm you and your unborn baby. Additionally, talk with your doctor if you are a breastfeeding mother because this supplement may pass into breast milk.

Contraindications

Do not use this product if you have the following diseases: Cancer; Hypotension (low blood pressure); Diabetes; Heart disease (including coronary artery disease, heart failure, recent heart attack, and others); Bleeding disorder.
This tablet contains natural ingredients. However, avoid using it if you have any sensitivities to its ingredients. Stop using the product and consult a healthcare professional if you have an allergic reaction.

Possible side effect

Possible side effects are not precisely established. Stomach and intestinal reactions are possible. Discuss with your healthcare professional for more details.

Drug interaction

This product may interact with some substances and medicines. It means that other drugs may change how Amalaki works by decreasing its effectiveness or even elevating your risk of side effects. Before using this supplement, inform your doctor about all products that you are using, especially: Anticoagulant or Antiplatelet drugs; Medicines used to treat diabetes; Aspirin; Clopidogrel.

Missed dose

If you miss a dose of Amalaki, take it as soon as possible. However, if it is almost time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and go back to your regular schedule. Do not double the dose.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical attention.

Storage

Keep the bottle tightly closed. Store it in a cool, dry place. Avoid direct heat and sunlight exposure. Do not refrigerate. Keep out of reach of children.
