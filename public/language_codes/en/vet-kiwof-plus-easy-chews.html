Common use

Kiwof Plus Easy Chews for small, medium and large dogs is a medicine used to treat and control various intestinal worm infections in dogs and cats. This includes roundworms, hookworms, whipworms, and certain tapeworms. It kills, eliminates, and prevents their growth and reproduction.

Dosage and direction

Give Kiwof Plus Easy Chews exactly as directed by your veterinarian, in the recommended dose and duration. It can usually be given with or without food, but administering it with food may help reduce stomach discomfort. Ensure your pet swallows the full dose, and repeat deworming as advised by your veterinarian to prevent reinfection.

Precautions

Use with caution in pregnant or lactating pets, and always follow the prescribed dose to avoid toxicity. Regular veterinary checkups and fecal examinations are important to ensure complete elimination of worms.

Contraindications

Do not use Kiwof Plus in weak or severely ill animals unless advised by a veterinarian. Avoid giving this medicine along with other deworming or anti-parasitic products unless specifically recommended by your vet.

Possible side effect

Kiwof Plus is generally well tolerated and does not usually cause any side effects. However, some pets may experience mild side effects such as vomiting, diarrhea, or loss of appetite. These effects are usually temporary and resolve on their own. If symptoms persist, worsen, or if your pet shows signs of an allergic reaction, such as swelling or difficulty breathing, contact your veterinarian immediately.

Drug interaction

Kiwof Plus Easy Chews must not be used simultaneously with other deworming medications, particularly those containing piperazine. It can generally be safely combined with other routine preventative treatments like flea and tick control, heartworm prevention, and vaccinations.

Missed dose

Give the medicine as soon as you can, but skip the missed dose if it is almost time for your next dose. Do not give two doses at one time.

Overdose

Seek your veterinarian for emergency medical attention.

Storage

Store at room temperature away from moisture and heat. Keep out of reach of the children and pets.
