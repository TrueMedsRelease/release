Common use

Pilex Forte Ointment is used for managing the discomfort associated with piles and haemorrhoids. This product is enriched with natural ingredients like Bhringaraja, Nirgundi, Jhendu and Lajjalu. These ingredients work together to help promote healing and reduce pain. This ointment may reduce the risk of infection. It may also support blood circulation and promote regular bowel movements.

Dosage and direction

Clean the affected area with lukewarm water. Apply a small amount of the ointment to the affected area. Use twice daily or as directed by a healthcare professional.

Precautions

For external use only. Avoid contact with eyes.
It is best to consult a healthcare professional before using this product during pregnancy.

Contraindications

Check for any allergies to the listed ingredients before use. In case you experience any side effects, discontinue use and consult your physician.

Possible side effect

Discontinue use and consult a healthcare professional if any adverse reactions occur.

Drug interaction

It's best to consult a healthcare professional before combining it with other treatments to avoid potential interactions.

Missed dose

If you forget to apply Pilex Forte Ointment at the scheduled time, do so as soon as you remember. However, if it's nearly time for the next application, skip the missed dose and return to your regular schedule. Never apply an extra quantity to make up for a missed dose without discussing it with your doctor.

Overdose

If you accidentally apply too much Pilex Forte Ointment, simply rinse the area with clean water. However, if symptoms such as mild irritation or discomfort persist, it's advisable to inform your doctor.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep out of reach of children.
