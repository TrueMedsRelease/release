Common use

Perfectil is a nutritional supplements designed to support skin, hair, and nail health by delivering micronutrients (including biotin, zinc, and selenium) directly to the deepest level through the bloodstream. It acts as a comprehensive daily multivitamin, with results for hair, skin, and nails typically noticed within 3–6 months of consistent, daily use.

Dosage and direction

One tablet daily after the main meal or as directed by the healthcare professional. Swallow with water. In case of difficulty in swallowing, break the tablet.

Precautions

Consult a doctor before use if you have epilepsy, a thyroid condition, or haemochromatosis.
Seek professional advice before taking Perfectil if you are pregnant or breastfeeding.

Contraindications

Contains soya and sometimes traces of fish (marine collagen), making them unsuitable for those with these allergies.
Not suitable for young children.

Possible side effect

Perfectil tablets are generally well-tolerated but can cause mild side effects like nausea, stomach upset, diarrhea, constipation, or headache.

Drug interaction

Those taking anticoagulants (blood thinners) should consult their doctor before using this product. Iron content may interact with certain medicines, including those for epilepsy (phenytoin) or thyroid issues.

Missed dose

Take the medicine as soon as you can, but skip the missed dose if it is almost time for your next dose. Do not take two doses at one time.

Overdose

An overdose of Perfectil tablets can lead to toxicity symptoms similar to iron or vitamin overload. Seek emergency medical attention.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry place below 25°Celsius. Keep away from direct sunlight, heat and moisture. Keep out of the reach of children and pets.
