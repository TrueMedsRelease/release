Common use

Pim-800 tablet is used in the treatment of Alzheimer's disease and memory loss in Parkinson's disease. It can also be used to treat age-related memory loss and head injury. It works by improving communication between the nerve cells and protects the brain. Pim-800 is also used for managing various neurological and cognitive disorders.

Dosage and direction

Take this medicine in the dose and duration as advised by your doctor. Swallow it as a whole. Do not chew, crush or break it. Pim-800 may be taken with or without food, but it is better to take it at a fixed time.

Precautions

Caution is advised when consuming alcohol with Pim-800. Please consult your doctor.
Information regarding the use of Pim-800 tablet during pregnancy or breastfeeding is not available. Please consult your doctor.
Pim-800 tablet should be used with caution in patients with kidney disease. Dose adjustment of Pim-800 may be needed. Please consult your doctor.

Contraindications

Pim-800 (piracetam) is contraindicated in patients with known allergies to any component of the formulation. It is also contraindicated in patients with severe renal impairment (creatinine clearance < 20 ml/min), cerebral haemorrhage, and those diagnosed with Huntington's disease.

Possible side effect

Side effects of Pim-800: Nervousness and anxiety; Sleep disturbances; Fatigue and drowsiness; Nausea and vomiting; Skin rash.

Drug interaction

Pim-800 (piracetam) may interact with anticoagulants (such as warfarin and heparin), and antiplatelet medications (like aspirin and clopidogrel).

Missed dose

If you miss a dose of Pim-800 tablet, take it as soon as possible. However, if it is almost time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and go back to your regular schedule. Do not double the dose.

Overdose

An overdose of Pim-800 may cause severe side effects such as anxiety, irritability, sleep disturbances, and gastrointestinal issues. If you suspect an overdose, consult your doctor immediately.
