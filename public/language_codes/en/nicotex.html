Common use

Nicotex Nicotine Gum is used for relieving nicotine cravings and managing your commitment to quitting smoking. This gum helps ensure a controlled release of nicotine, helping to reduce the severity of withdrawal symptoms and cravings without the harmful gases associated with smoking. Nicotex Chewing Gum provides small and sustained quantities of nicotine, helping you to receive the necessary support to overcome the challenges of quitting smoking.

Dosage and direction

Chew the gum slowly till you get the taste of nicotine. Place and rest between your cheeks and gums. When the taste fades, repeat step 1.

Precautions

Consult your healthcare provider before using Nicotex gum if you are pregnant or breastfeeding, as nicotine can affect your baby.
Individuals with heart disease, high blood pressure, or diabetes should exercise caution when using Nicotine replacement therapy and consult their doctor before use.

Contraindications

Nicotine replacement therapy is contraindicated in individuals with known allergies to nicotine. It is also contraindicated for non-smokers, occasional smokers, and individuals with unstable cardiovascular conditions such as a recent heart attack, severe arrhythmia or recent stroke.

Possible side effect

Some users may experience mild side effects like mouth irritation, hiccups, or an upset stomach, headache. If severe side effects occur, stop using the gum and consult a healthcare provider.

Drug interaction

This medicine may interact with antidepressants (such as bupropion and nortriptyline), antipsychotics (such as clozapine and olanzapine), benzodiazepines (like diazepam and alprazolam), and beta-blockers (such as atenolol and propranolol).

Missed dose

If you miss a dose of Nicotine replacement therapy product, take it as soon as you remember. However, avoid doubling up on doses to make up for a missed one.

Overdose

Overdosing on Nicotex can cause symptoms such as palpitations, nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, sweating, headache, dizziness, hearing problems, and confusion. In severe cases, it could lead to seizures and even death. If you suspect an overdose, seek immediate medical attention.

Storage

Keep out of the reach of children. Store at room temperature. Protect from excessive heat and direct sunlight.
