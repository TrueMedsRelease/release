Common use

Sildigra Gold tablets are designed to treat Erectile Dysfunction ED), a condition characterized by the difficulty or inability to obtain or sustain an erection sufficient for sexual activity. These tablets contain Sildenafil Citrate. This high-dose formulation is often prescribed for individuals who require a more robust solution to achieve satisfactory sexual performance.

Dosage and direction

Follow the instructions mentioned on the product label or by your doctor. Here are the general guidelines for using Sildigra Gold: Take it 30-60 minutes before planned intercourse. Take Sildigra orally with a glass of water. Do not crush or chew the tablet. Do not take more than one tablet a day.

Precautions

Sildigra Gold may not be safe for individuals with liver diseases. Sildigra Gold may be used in kidney patients under medical supervision, but dosage needs to be strictly monitored. Sildigra Gold is intended for men and is not suitable for women.
Grapefruit or grapefruit juice may elevate Sildenafil levels in your bloodstream. Excessive alcohol consumption may also worsen ED and heighten the risk of side effects.

Contraindications

Sildigra Gold is contraindicated in patients with a history of hypersensitivity to Sildenafil. It is also contraindicated in patients with pulmonary veno-occlusive disease, stomach ulcer, heart disease, kidney disease, liver disease, patients who had a heart attack, heart failure, angina, irregular heartbeat, stroke, chest pain, high or low blood pressure, high cholesterol, bleeding disorder, blood circulation problems, blood cell problems such as sickle cell anemia, multiple myeloma, leukemia, eye disease or diabetes.

Possible side effect

Side effects of Sildigra Gold: Headache; Heartburn; Diarrhea; Nausea; Flushing; Nosebleeds; Pain in muscles, arms, and legs.

Drug interaction

Certain medications can interact with Sildigra Gold. Here are some categories of medications and specific examples: Nitrates: Nitroglycerin, Isosorbide dinitrate; Alpha-blockers: Tamsulosin, Doxazosin; Antifungals: Ketoconazole, Itraconazole; Antibiotics: Erythromycin, Clarithromycin.

Missed dose

If you forget to take Sildigra Gold as instructed, take it as soon as you remember. Do not take more than one tablet a day.

Overdose

If you accidentally take too much Sildigra Gold, seek immediate medical attention. Taking too much of this medicine can lead to severe side effects such as a prolonged and painful erection (priapism). Contact your doctor or visit a nearby hospital if you experience any discomfort or unusual symptoms.
