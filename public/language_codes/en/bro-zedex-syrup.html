Common use

Bro-Zedex Syrup is a medicine that helps manage cough with mucus. It is designed to make it easier for you to cough out the excessive mucus present in your nose, windpipe, and lungs. Bro-Zedex Syrup is commonly used to manage conditions such as bronchitis, bronchial asthma, chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), bronchiectasis, and emphysema.

Dosage and direction

Shake the bottle well before use. Consult your doctor for the appropriate dosage and duration of treatment. You can take this medicine with or without food. It is recommended to take it at the same time every day for optimal results.

Precautions

Patients with hypertension, heart disease, diabetes mellitus, hyperthyroidism, glaucoma, and liver or kidney impairment need to exercise extra caution when using Bro-Zedex Syrup.
If you are pregnant or breastfeeding, please always consult your doctor before using this medicine in these conditions.

Contraindications

Bro-Zedex Syrup is contraindicated in patients with a known hypersensitivity to any of the individual ingredients (Bromhexine, Terbutaline, Guaifenesin, Menthol) or sympathomimetic amines like tyramine and ephedrine.
Bro-Zedex Syrup is not suitable for individuals with tachyarrhythmia, hypertrophic obstructive cardiomyopathy, or hyperthyroidism.

Possible side effect

Side effects of Bro-Zedex Syrup: Nausea; Vomiting; Headache; Dizziness; Dry mouth.

Drug interaction

Bro-Zedex Syrup may interact with beta-blockers (such as propranolol and atenolol), diuretics (such as furosemide and hydrochlorothiazide), Monoamine Oxidase Inhibitors (MAOIs like phenelzine and selegiline), and tricyclic antidepressants (like amitriptyline and nortriptyline).

Missed dose

If you forget a dose, take it as soon as you remember. If it's almost time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and continue with your regular dosing schedule.

Overdose

Taking too much Bro-Zedex Syrup can result in an overdose. Symptoms may include nausea, vomiting, stomach pain, headache, tremors, increased heart rate, and skin rash. If you or someone else accidentally takes too much, seek immediate medical attention by contacting your doctor or visiting a nearby hospital.

Storage

Store Bro-Zedex Syrup at room temperature. Keep the medicine away from direct sunlight and moisture. Ensure that the bottle is tightly closed after each use. Keep this product out of the reach of children and pets.
