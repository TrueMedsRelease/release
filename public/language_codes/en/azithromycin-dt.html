Common use

Azithromycin DT is an antibiotic used to treat bacterial infections like bronchitis, pneumonia, STDs, chest infections, ear, nose, and throat infections such as sinusitis, respiratory infections, ear infections, and skin infections. It belongs to the macrolide class and is also used in combination therapies for diseases like Mycobacterium avium complex (MAC) in HIV-infected patients.

Dosage and direction

Swallow the tablet whole with a glass of water. Avoid crushing or chewing the tablet. It should be taken either 1 hour before or 2 hours after a meal or as advised by doctor. Do not stop taking this medicine without speaking to your doctor first.

Precautions

Liver Disease: If you have liver disease, azithromycin can potentially exacerbate your condition.
Kidney Disease: People with kidney problems should be careful as Azithromycin DT might worsen their condition.
Long QT Syndrome: This medication may cause an irregular heartbeat in people with this heart condition.

Contraindications

Azithromycin DT is contraindicated in patients with known allergies to azithromycin and severe heart disease and other infection.

Possible side effect

The side effects of Azithromycin DT are: Diarrhoea; Headache; Vomiting; Stomach pain; Loss of appetite.

Drug interaction

The utilisation of antiemetic medications (domperidone), antipsychotic medications (amitriptyline, pimozide), antimigraine medications (ergotamine, dihydroergotamine), statin medications (simvastatin), medications for bladder weakness (tolterodine), and anti-gout/arthritis medications (colchicine) in conjunction with Azithromycin DT is not advised due to the potential for severe side effects. Before using any prescription or over-the-counter medications, consultation with a doctor is essential.

Missed dose

If you forget to take a dose of Azithromycin DT, take it as soon as you remember, but avoid repeating it.

Overdose

Overdose of Azithromycin DT can cause loss of hearing, feeling sick or sick, and diarrhoea. Inform your doctor and follow the advice accordingly.

Storage

Store Azithromycin DT in a dry place, protected from light and moisture. Maintain an ideal room temperature between 15°C and 30°C. Do not expose the medicine to high temperatures or freezing conditions. Always ensure that your medicine is kept out of the reach of children. Use the medicine before its expiry date.
