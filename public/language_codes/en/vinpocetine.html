Common use

Vinpocetine is a synthetic compound derived from the periwinkle plant, used primarily as a nootropic supplement to enhance cognitive function, memory, and focus. It is widely used to improve blood circulation to the brain, treat cerebrovascular diseases, aid in stroke recovery, and manage dementia. Vinpocetine is also used to enhance memory and increase brain metabolism.

Dosage and direction

It is generally taken as oral tablets. It is recommended to take vinpocetine after meals, as this can improve its bioavailability. For best results, it is often used regularly over a sustained period, as effects may take weeks to months to become apparent. Use between 5 and 30 mg vinpocetine given up to 3 times daily.

Precautions

Use cautiously if you have bleeding disorders, low blood pressure, or are scheduling surgery. Vinpocetine may increase the risk of bruising and bleeding. Discontinue use at least 2 weeks before any scheduled surgery.

Contraindications

Vinpocetine is contraindicated in pregnant women (or those planning pregnancy) due to risks of miscarriage and fetal harm, as advised by the FDA. It is also contraindicated in individuals with known hypersensitivity to it, bleeding disorders, recent strokes (hemorrhagic), and severe heart problems.

Possible side effect

Potential side effects include dizziness, headaches, nausea, anxiety, insomnia, and dry mouth.

Drug interaction

Increased risks when combined with warfarin (Coumadin), clopidogrel (Plavix), and NSAIDs like aspirin, ibuprofen (Advil), and naproxen (Aleve); Use with caution if taking medications for high blood pressure or heart conditions, as it may increase their effects, leading to hypotension or heart rhythm issues.

Missed dose

Take the missed dose as soon as you remember. If it is almost time for your next dose, skip the missed dose. Do not double your dose to make up for the missed one.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical treatment or contact the doctor in case of an overdose.

Storage

Vinpocetine should be stored in a cool, dry, well-ventilated area, in original container, protected from light and moisture. Keep away from children and pets.
