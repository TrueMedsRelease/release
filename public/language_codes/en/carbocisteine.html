Common use

Carbocisteine use to manage respiratory conditions characterised by excessive mucus production such as chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD), chronic bronchitis, and cystic fibrosis. It belongs to the group of mucolytic medicines. It also helps to thin and loosen the thick, sticky mucus characteristic of cystic fibrosis, improving patients' ability to clear their airways.

Dosage and direction

Carbocisteine is available as an capsules are to be taken by mouth. Generally safe to take Carbocisteine daily as long as it is taken according to your doctor's recommendations. Carbocisteine starts working soon after being taken, but it may take some time to notice an improvement in symptoms. Continue taking the medication as prescribed even if effects are not immediately apparent. Carbocisteine is not associated with weight gain. If you experience significant weight changes while using this medicine, please consult your doctor.

Precautions

Use in Pregnancy and Lactation: It is advised to consult your doctor for advice before using this medicine during these periods.
Caution should be exercised when using Carbocisteine in elderly individuals. If you have any pre-existing health conditions, ensure to inform your doctor before starting this medication.

Contraindications

Carbocisteine is contraindicated in patients with known hypersensitivity to the medicine or any of its components.
It should also not be used in patients with active peptic ulcer disease, as it can potentially exacerbate the condition.

Possible side effect

Side effects of Carbocisteine: Nausea; Vomiting; Diarrhea; Abdominal pain; Allergic reactions.

Drug interaction

Carbocisteine may interact with antibiotics (such as amoxicillin, ciprofloxacin, and doxycycline).

Missed dose

In case you miss a dose of Carbocisteine, take it as soon as you remember. However, if it is nearly time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and continue with your regular dosing schedule.

Overdose

Overdosing on Carbocisteine may lead to symptoms like nausea, vomiting, abdominal discomfort, and diarrhea. If you suspect an overdose, immediately consult a doctor for advice.

Storage

Store at room temperature away from moisture, heat, and light. Keep away from children and pets.
