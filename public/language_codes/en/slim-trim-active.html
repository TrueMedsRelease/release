Common use

Slim Trim Active capsule is primarily used to manage obesity, working effectively as part of a comprehensive weight management programm. This medication belongs to a class of medicines known as lipase inhibitors. In addition to managing obesity, this capsule helps overweight individuals lose excess body weight and maintain the weight loss. Reducing body weight can lessen health risks like diabetes, high blood pressure, and heart disease and improve your overall quality of life.

Dosage and direction

Swallow the capsule whole with a glass of water. The medicine can be taken during or within 1 hour after each main meal that contains fat. If a meal is missed or does not contain fat, skip your dose of Slim Trim Active. For optimal results, evenly distribute your daily intake of fat, protein, and carbohydrates over three main meals.

Precautions

Slim Trim Active should be avoided if you are pregnant. It is best to discuss this with your doctor before taking this medicine.
Breastfeeding mothers should either discontinue using Slim Trim Active capsule or cease breastfeeding. Consult your doctor to understand the potential risks and benefits.
If you have liver problems, use caution when taking Slim Trim Active. Consult your doctor, who will provide guidance based on your health status.
Caution is advised before using Slim Trim Active if you have a history of kidney problems. Consult your doctor before taking this medicine.
Slim Trim Active is not recommended for children under 12 years.

Contraindications

Slim Trim Active is contraindicated in patients with known allergies to the Orlistat medicine. It is best avoided by those with gallbladder problems, chronic malabsorption syndrome (a digestive disorder that prevents the body from absorbing nutrients from food) or cholestasis (liver dysfunction that occurs when bile secretion and flow are reduced or stagnant).

Possible side effect

While taking Slim Trim Active capsule, you might experience some of the following side effects: Oily spotting in feces; Flatus with discharge: This refers to passing gas that may be accompanied by a small amount of fluid or feces matter; feces urgency: You may feel a sudden, strong urge to pass stool; Increased defecation: You might need to go to the toilet more often than usual; Soft stools.

Drug interaction

Anticoagulants: Orlistat can interfere with the absorption of vitamin K, which can enhance the effects of anticoagulants like warfarin, increasing the risk of bleeding.
Antidiabetic Medications: Orlistat may affect blood glucose levels, potentially altering the effectiveness of antidiabetic medications like metformin.
Thyroid Hormones: Orlistat can decrease the absorption of levothyroxine, potentially leading to hypothyroidism.
Antiepileptic Medicines: The absorption of antiepileptic medicines such as valproate or lamotrigine may be reduced when taken with orlistat, possibly leading to decreased seizure control.

Missed dose

If you forget to take a dose of Slim Trim Active, take it as soon as you remember. If it's nearly time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and follow your regular schedule. Never increase your dosage without consulting your doctor.

Overdose

If you accidentally take more Slim Trim Active than prescribed, you might experience symptoms like oily spotting in feces, flatus with discharge, or increased defecation. If these symptoms persist or worsen, consult your doctor.

Storage

Store Slim Trim Active capsule in a cool, dry place, ideally at a temperature below 30 degrees C. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight or heat. Keep it tightly sealed in its original packaging. Always keep this medicine out of reach of children. Ensure you use Slim Trim Active before the expiry date printed on the pack.
