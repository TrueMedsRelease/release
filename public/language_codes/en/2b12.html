Common use

2b12 tablets is used for supporting nerve health, energy production, and red blood cell formation. It contains a blend of Adenosylcobalamin, Methylcobalamin, Folic Acid, Vitamin B1, Vitamin B2, and Vitamin B6, which work together to manage optimal nervous system function, help in DNA synthesis, and support cardiovascular health. This supplement is particularly beneficial for individuals experiencing vitamin B12 deficiency, fatigue, or neurological concerns. Regular intake helps maintain metabolic balance, manages brain function, and reduces the risk of anaemia.

Dosage and direction

Follow the specific instructions provided by your doctor or pharmacist, as the appropriate amount can vary based on individual needs. Swallow whole with a drink of water; do not chew, crush, or break the tablet. It is best to take them on an empty stomach for optimal absorption.

Precautions

2B12 tablets is generally considered safe for use during pregnancy and breastfeeding under medical supervision. However, it is always advised to consult your doctor before starting any medication during these periods.
Caution should be exercised when using this combination in patients with kidney or liver disease, as these conditions may affect the metabolism and activation of these nutrients.

Contraindications

2B12 tablets should not be used in patients with known hypersensitivity to any of its components. It is also contraindicated in individuals with a rare genetic condition known as Leber's disease (hereditary optic nerve atrophy).

Possible side effect

Side effects of 2B12 tablets: Nausea, Diarrhea, Stomach cramps, Headache, Skin rash.

Drug interaction

This combination may interact with anticonvulsants (such as phenytoin, phenobarbital, and primidone) and diabetes medication (like metformin).

Missed dose

If you miss a dose, take it as soon as you remember. However, if it is near the time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and go back to your regular schedule. Do not double the dose to compensate for the missed one.

Overdose

Overdose of this nutrient combination may cause symptoms such as nausea, diarrhea, stomach cramps, or rash. If you experience these symptoms or any other unusual changes after taking this medicine, consult your doctor immediately.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry and dark place. Keep away from direct sunlight, heat and moisture. Keep out of the reach of children.
