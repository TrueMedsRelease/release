Common use

Calcibrook Forte tablets is used for supporting healthy calcium and magnesium levels in the body. It contains calcium carbonate, which may assist in maintaining bone health, while magnesium helps contribute to the proper functioning of bones and joints. The inclusion of zinc also supports the body’s nutrient balance.

Dosage and direction

Take one tablet per day or as directed by your healthcare professional. Do not chew or crush the tablet. Swallow it as a whole with water. It is recommended to be consumed after meals.

Precautions

Pregnant or lactating women should consult a healthcare professional before using the Calcibrook Forte. While the ingredients in this product are commonly used to support bone and joint health, individual recommendations from a healthcare provider are essential to ensure safety during pregnancy or breastfeeding. People with various medical conditions should consult a doctor before using.

Contraindications

Calcibrook Forte should be avoided if you have hypercalcemia (high blood calcium), hypercalciuria (high calcium in urine), severe kidney disease, or kidney stones. It is contraindicated if you are allergic to any components: Calcium Carbonate, Magnesium Hydroxide, Zinc Sulphate Monohydrate.

Possible side effect

Calcibrook Forte is generally well tolerated when taken as directed. However, some individuals may experience mild digestive discomfort such as bloating or constipation. If you experience any unusual side effects, it's important to consult a healthcare provider for guidance.

Drug interaction

Other drugs may affect Calcibrook Forte, including prescription and over-the-counter medicines, vitamins, and herbal products. Tell your doctor about all other medicines you use.

Missed dose

Take the medicine as soon as you can, but skip the missed dose if it is almost time for your next dose. Do not take two doses at one time.

Overdose

Do not exceed the recommended dosage. If an overdose is suspected, contact emergency services immediately.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry and dark place. Keep away from direct sunlight, heat and moisture. Keep out of the reach of children and pets.
