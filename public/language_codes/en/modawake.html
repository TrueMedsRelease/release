Common use

Modawake promotes wakefulness and is used to treat excessive daytime sleepiness caused by narcolepsy, obstructive sleep apnea (OSA), or shift work sleep (SWS) disorder.

Dosage and direction

Take Modawake exactly as prescribed by your doctor. Do not take this medicine in larger or smaller amounts or for longer than recommended. Usual Adult Dose - Modawake 200 mg orally daily in the morning.

Precautions

Modawake should be used during pregnancy only if the potential benefit justifies the potential risk to the foetus. Breastfeeding mothers should consult their doctor before using this medication.
Individuals with severe hepatic impairment should take Modawake with caution as dosage adjustment may be required.
Patients with a history of psychiatric conditions, such as depression or psychosis, should also exercise caution when taking Modawake.

Contraindications

Modawake is contraindicated in individuals with known hypersensitivity or allergic reactions to the medicine or any of its components. It is also not suggested for those with a history of left ventricular hypertrophy (an abnormal thickening of the heart muscle).

Possible side effect

Side effects of Modawake: Headache; Nausea; Anxiety; Insomnia; Dizziness.

Drug interaction

Modawake may interact with certain types of medications, such as antidepressants (fluoxetine and sertraline), medications used to treat Parkinson's disease (pramipexole), and certain antifungals (such as ketoconazole and itraconazole).

Missed dose

In case you forget to take your dose of Modawake, take it as soon as you remember. However, if it is near the time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and continue with your regular dosing schedule.

Overdose

Overdosing on Modawake can result in symptoms like insomnia, restlessness, confusion, disorientation, and excitement. Should you suspect an overdose, immediately consult your doctor.

Storage

Store Modawake tablets at room temperature away from moisture and heat. Keep away from children.
