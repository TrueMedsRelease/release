Common use

Keraglo Eva tablet is a nutritional supplement formulated for women to combat hair fall, strengthen follicles, and improve hair texture and thickness. It contains a blend of nutrients like Gamma-Linolenic Acid, antioxidants (grape seed/green tea extract), vitamins, and minerals that nourish the scalp, promote healthy growth, and reduce damage.

Dosage and direction

For best results, take one tablet daily, with water, preferably at the same time each day, either before or after a meal. Do not crush or chew the tablet; swallow it whole.

Precautions

Consult a doctor if you have underlying conditions, particularly iron overload disorders or copper metabolism disorders. Pregnant or lactating women should consult a doctor before using.

Contraindications

Keraglo Eva tablets should be avoided or used with caution if you have known allergies to any components or have pre-existing liver or kidney conditions. Consult a doctor before use, especially if taking other medications.

Possible side effect

Keraglo Eva may cause mild, temporary side effects, including nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, stomach discomfort, and headaches. Some users may experience skin rash, dizziness, or rare allergic reactions.

Drug interaction

Keraglo Eva tablets may interact with medications like carbamazepine, phenobarbital, ciprofloxacin, and barbiturates. It is crucial to inform your doctor about all current medications, including supplements, to avoid potential interactions.

Missed dose

Take the medicine as soon as you can, but skip the missed dose if it is almost time for your next dose. Do not take two doses at one time.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical attention.

Storage

Store in a cool and dry place. Keep away from direct sunlight. Keep away from children and pets.
