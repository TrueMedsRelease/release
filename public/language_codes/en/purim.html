Common use

Purim tablet is used for detoxifying the body using neem and turmeric. It helps in the management of acute and chronic dermatitis. It helps combat acne and relieve skin allergies. It supports liver health and helps in purifying the blood, which can improve skin condition. This tablet helps manage occasional digestive issues and provides antioxidant protection against free radicals. Regular use contributes to overall well-being and a healthier body.

Dosage and direction

Take 1-2 tablets twice daily after meals or as directed by your healthcare provider. Swallow the tablets with water.

Precautions

Consult with a healthcare provider before use, especially if you are pregnant, nursing, or have any medical conditions.

Contraindications

Contains no common allergens. In case of an allergic reaction, please consult a healthcare provider and stop using it immediately. Do not exceed the recommended dosage.

Possible side effect

Side effects are rare but can include mild digestive discomfort. Discontinue use if any adverse reactions occur and consult with a healthcare provider.

Drug interaction

Consult with your healthcare provider to ensure there are no interactions with other supplements or medications you may be taking.
