Common use

Vidalista Black is a potent medication designed to treat Erectile Dysfunction (ED) or impotence in men. Its active ingredient is Tadalafil, a Phosphodiesterase type 5 or PDE5 inhibitor.
Vidalista Black containing Tadalafil relieves Erectile Dysfunction symptoms by increasing blood circulation to your penile area resulting in longer lasting and firmer erections.
Besides ED, Vidalista Black may also be recommended by doctors to manage conditions like Benign Prostatic Hyperplasia (BPH) and Pulmonary Arterial Hypertension (PAH).

Dosage and direction

Take one tablet about 30-60 minutes before planned sexual activity. Swallow it whole with water, avoiding chewing, crushing, or breaking. It can be consumed with or without food, but taking it on an empty stomach may enhance the effectiveness. Do not exceed one tablet in 24 hours. Always consult your doctor before taking or stopping Vidalista Black to ensure safe and effective use.

Precautions

Some of the essential points you should note before using Vidalista Black include the following:
Males above the age of 18 only should use it.
You should not be allergic to Tadalafil or any other component present in this medicine.
Do not start/ stop using this medicine without consulting your doctor.
This medication is not suggested for female patients.

Contraindications

Liver Disease: Individuals with liver conditions may require lower doses and regular monitoring.
Kidney Impairment: Those with kidney problems should use Vidalista Black cautiously and with medical supervision.
Avoid: Steer clear of grapefruit juice, alcohol, and medications like nitrates or alpha-blockers, as these may increase the risk of side effects such as dizziness or fainting.
Do not take Vidalista Black with nitrates and alphablockers: It could cause you to become dizzy, faint, or even have a heart attack.

Possible side effect

Vidalista Black is associated with specific side effects, which are generally mild, but if you experience any severe side effects, consult your doctor. Some of the side effects include: Headache; Nausea; Flushing; Diarrhea; Problems with vision; Dizziness; Painful urination.

Drug interaction

Consulting a doctor before using Vidalista Black is always recommended. Certain drugs can interact with Vidalista Black and cause side effects. Some of the drugs that are known to interact with Vidalista Black include:
Amlodipine
Carbamazepine
Ketoconazole
Phenytoin
Nitroglycerin
Cimetidine
Riociguat
Other ED medications

Missed dose

Take a dose as prescribed by a doctor and try not to miss any dose. But if you miss any dose, try to avoid taking an extra amount or two doses in one go to compensate for the missed one. Skip the missed dose.

Overdose

Do not take an overdose of Vidalista Black; if you take an overdose by mistake, rush to your nearest hospital immediately. Overdose can also cause certain complications, therefore consult the doctor immediately.

Storage

Please keep it away from children and pets. Store Vidalista Black in a clean, dry place. Do not freeze it.
