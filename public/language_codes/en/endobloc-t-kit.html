Common use

Endobloc T Kit tablet is a combination of two active ingredients called, Ambrisentan and Tadalafil, used in treating pulmonary arterial hypertension (PAH). It functions by inducing relaxation in the blood vessels within the lungs.

Dosage and direction

Take the Endobloc T Kit tablet as your physician advises. Usually, the tablets will be taken with or without food. Do not crush, chew, or open the medicine. Your physician will decide the correct dosage and duration based on age, body weight, and disease condition. Only stop taking the tablet if your doctor advises you to stop.

Precautions

Before starting to consume Endobloc T Kit tablet, inform your doctor if you have hypotension, a history of heart failure, or severe liver disease, if you recently had a stroke or myocardial infarction, if you have or have had lung conditions, anemia, or edema (fluid retention).
Before beginning the treatment, notify your doctor if you have any issues such as penis deformation, swelling in the extremities, prolonged or painful erection (priapism), cancer (leukemia, multiple myeloma), severe kidney problems, abnormality of red blood cells, or loss of hearing.

Contraindications

Do not consume this medicine if you are allergic to Ambrisentan, Tadalafil or any of its ingredients in the medication. Avoid taking this medicine if you have liver conditions or low blood pressure.
Do not consume this medicine if you are pregnant or breastfeeding. It is unsafe to consume Endobloc T Kit tablet if you are pregnant because it may cause harm to the unborn baby.

Possible side effect

Common side effect: Redness of skin; Vomiting; Chest pain; Ringing in the ears; Abnormal liver function; Constipation.

Drug interaction

Inform your doctor about any prescribed medications and over-the-counter medicines. Certain medications, such as Riociguat, Ritonavir, Ketoconazole, and alpha-blockers, may interact with Endobloc T Kit tablet and can cause undesirable side effects.

Missed dose

If you miss a Endobloc T Kit tablet dose, take it as soon as possible. However, if it's close to the time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and continue with your regular schedule. Do not double the dose.

Overdose

It can be danger for you health and life, seek emergency medical attention.

Storage

Do not store above 30°C. Do not freeze the medicine. Keep out of sight and reach of children and pets.
