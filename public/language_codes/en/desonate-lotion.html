Common use

Desonate Lotion is specially formulated to manage various skin conditions such as eczema, psoriasis, and dermatitis. It helps reduce symptoms like redness, itching, and swelling in the affected areas, providing relief from skin discomfort. This lotion is intended for external use only and should be applied as prescribed by your healthcare provider.

Dosage and direction

Clean the affected area before applying. Wash your hands before and after use, unless treating hands. Apply as directed by your doctor or healthcare professional. Avoid contact with eyes; rinse with water if accidental contact occurs. Use for the prescribed duration as advised by your doctor.

Precautions

Desonate Lotion should be used during pregnancy and breastfeeding only if the potential benefit justifies the potential risk to the foetus. Consult your doctor before using this medicine during pregnancy and lactation.
Patients with skin infections, rosacea, acne or perioral dermatitis should exercise caution when using Desonate Lotion.

Contraindications

This medicine is contraindicated in patients with a known allergy to Desonide or any of its components. It is also contraindicated in patients with infected skin lesions caused by bacteria, fungi, or viruses.

Possible side effect

Desonate Lotion may cause side effects such as skin irritation, including stinging, burning, or itching at the application site. Some people may experience dryness or slight peeling of the skin. If these side effects worsen or persist, consult your healthcare provider.

Drug interaction

Desonate Lotion (Desonide) may interact with other topical corticosteroids (e.g., hydrocortisone, prednisone), and immunosuppressants (e.g., tacrolimus, cyclosporin).

Missed dose

If you forget to apply Desonate Lotion, apply it as soon as you remember. But avoid applying extra to make up for a missed dose.

Overdose

Overdose of Desonate Lotion is unlikely as it is intended for external use only. Using the prescribed dosage is recommended to avoid any side effects.

Storage

Store Desonate Lotion at room temperature. Keep it away from direct sunlight, moisture, and heat. Make sure to tightly close the bottle when not in use. Protect the medicine from freezing or extreme temperatures. Keep it out of reach and sight of children and pets.
