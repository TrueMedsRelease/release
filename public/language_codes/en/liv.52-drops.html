Dosage and direction

Liv.52 Oral Drops are generally safe for daily use when taken as per recommended dosage or as directed by a healthcare provider. Consult a healthcare provider for personalized instructions.

Precautions

Consult a physician to determine the appropriate dosage and safety for children.
Pregnant or lactating women and people with other medical conditions should consult a doctor before using.
Avoid excessive alcohol consumption to support liver health.

Contraindications

Contains natural / homoeopathic ingredients. However, if you have any sensitivities to its ingredients, avoid using it. In case of an allergic reaction, stop using the product and consult a healthcare professional.

Possible side effect

Liv.52 Drops are generally safe for use. However, some individuals may experience mild digestive upset. It is always recommended to consult with a healthcare provider if any discomfort arises. Side effects are rare but may include mild nausea or stomach irritation in sensitive individuals.

Drug interaction

It is advised to consult your healthcare provider before combining Liv.52 with other medications.

Missed dose

If you miss a dose of Liv.52 Drops, take it as soon as possible. However, if it is almost time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and go back to your regular schedule. Do not double the dose.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical attention.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep out of reach of children.
