Common use

Super Vilitra is a combination of two drugs Vardenafil and Dapoxetine into one and is used as a treatment for two conditions: erectile dysfunctions (impotence) and premature ejaculation. Vardenafil is a phosphodiesterase type 5 inhibitor (PDE5 inhibitor) while Dapoxetine is a selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor (SSRI).

Dosage and direction

Super Vilitra tablets are to be used if and when you want to have sexual intercourse with your partner. Only one dosage is to be taken in 24 hours, if you want another dosage, please wait for at least 24 hours. You have to gulp down the whole tablet with water, without chewing or breaking it. You can eat food along with it, but do not consume very fatty meals along with this medication because it will delay the onset period of this medication. This oral erectile dysfunction and premature ejaculation treatment starts to kick in your system within 30 minutes to 1 hour and stays effective for 5 to 6 hours. Please do not exceed or alter the prescribed dosage to prevent the risk of overdose.

Precautions

If you suffering from low blood pressure, please inform your doctor about it. As this medication relaxes the blood vessels of your body, your blood pressure may dip, therefore, your doctor may have to monitor your health after you use this medication.
Completely avoid alcohol or drink only in moderation while these drugs are in your system as they can lower your blood pressure, cause dizziness, and even make you faint.
If you suffer from cardiovascular diseases, diseases that affect the liver, kidney, and eyes, and bleeding disorders such as Hemophilia and Von Willebrand disease then you might need your doctor to adjust your dosage of Super Vilitra based on the severity of your condition as well as your medical records. If you suffer from any other diseases or genetic conditions be sure to tell your doctor to rule out the possibility of making it worse with this drug.

Contraindications

You should not use Super Vilitra if you are allergic to Vardenafil or Dapoxetine.
Super Vilitra tablets are a combination of Vardenafil and Dapoxetine, hence, do not use any medication, or substance that contains any of these two ingredients, as doing so will increase the dosage and make you vulnerable to overdose, and give you serious side effects.

Possible side effect

Common side effects of Super Vilitra may include: flushing (sudden warmth, redness, or tingly feeling); stuffy nose, sinus pain; headache, dizziness; upset stomach; back pain; agitation and disorientation; extreme sweating; diarrhea; insomnia; blurred eyesight; buzzing or humming in the ears.

Drug interaction

Do not take Super Vilitra if you are also using a nitrate drug for chest pain or heart problems, including nitroglycerin, isosorbide dinitrate, and isosorbide mononitrate. Nitrates are also found in some recreational drugs such as amyl nitrate or nitrite ("poppers"). Taking Super Vilitra with a nitrate medicine can cause a sudden and serious decrease in blood pressure.

Missed dose

Since Super Vilitra is used as needed, you are not likely to be on a dosing schedule. Do not take Super Vilitra more than once a day. Allow 24 hours to pass between doses.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical attention.

Storage

Store at room temperature away from moisture and heat. Keep away from children.
