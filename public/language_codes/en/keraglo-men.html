Common use

Keraglo Men tablets helps manage hair fall and promote hair growth in men. It nourishes hair follicles, improves scalp health, and strengthens hair, addressing thinning and weak strands for healthier, stronger hair. Enriched with essential vitamins, minerals, and antioxidants, it supports keratin production, improves hair texture, and reduces breakage. Regular use may also help control dandruff, enhance hair thickness, and restore natural shine, making it a comprehensive supplement for men’s hair wellness.

Dosage and direction

Take one tablet daily, swallowed whole with water, either before or after a meal. Do not crush, break, or chew the tablet. Use consistently for 3–6 months for optimal results, or as advised by a doctor.

Precautions

Consult a doctor before starting, especially if you have liver, kidney, or bleeding disorders. Do not exceed the recommended daily dose.

Contraindications

Do not use if you have a known hypersensitivity to any of the ingredients in the tablet, including milk or soy. It contains ingredients that may not be suitable for those with lactose intolerance (contains Lactose Monohydrate).

Possible side effect

Common side effects: Gastrointestinal issues (nausea, vomiting, diarrhea, stomach upset), headache, and skin rash or itching.

Drug interaction

Potential interactions may exist with anticonvulsants (Phenytoin, Phenobarbital) and corticosteroids (Prednisone). Consult a doctor before combining it with other medications.

Missed dose

If a dose is missed, take it as soon as remembered. Skip it if it is almost time for the next dose; do not double the dose.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical attention.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight, moisture and heat. Keep out of the reach of children and pets.
