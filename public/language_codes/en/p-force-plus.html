Common use

P-Force Plus is a medication designed to treat Erectile Dysfunction (ED), a common issue impacting many men. It belongs to the class of Phosphodiesterase type 5 inhibitors. The active ingredient in this Erectile Dysfunction solution is Sildenafil Citrate, which works by enhancing penile blood flow and helping men have and keep an erection during sexual activity. This makes it a successful sexual performance booster and sexual vitality enhancement tool.

Dosage and direction

Take P-Force Plus about 30 minutes to 1 hour before sexual activity. Swallow the tablet whole with water. For Erectile Dysfunction, it is best to take P-Force Plus on an empty stomach. Taking P-Force Plus with a high-fat meal may delay the onset of action. Avoid taking more than one tablet per day. Use as needed, not for daily consumption.

Precautions

An individual with liver issue should consult their doctor before using P-Force Plus, as it may require dose adjustments. If you have severe liver disease, your doctor may advise against using Sildenafil or recommend alternative treatments.
Those with kidney issues should also consult a healthcare provider to ensure safe use. For mild to moderate renal impairment, no dose adjustment is usually required for Erectile Dysfunction, but it's still important to consult your doctor.
P-Force Plus is not intended for women. Women who are pregnant, breastfeeding, or may become pregnant should avoid exposure to P-Force Plus.

Contraindications

P-Force Plus is contraindicated in patients with a history of hypersensitivity to Sildenafil. It is also contraindicated in patients with pulmonary veno-occlusive disease, stomach ulcer, heart disease, kidney disease, liver disease, patients who had a heart attack, heart failure, angina, irregular heartbeat, stroke, chest pain, high or low blood pressure, high cholesterol, bleeding disorder, blood circulation problems, blood cell problems such as sickle cell anemia, multiple myeloma, leukemia, eye disease or diabetes.

Possible side effect

Common P-Force Plus side effects include: Headache; Dizziness; Nausea; Upset stomach; Vision changes; Flushing; Pain in muscles, arms, and legs.

Drug interaction

Certain medications can interact with P-Force Plus. Here are some categories of medications and specific examples: Nitrates: Nitroglycerin, Isosorbide dinitrate; Alpha-blockers: Tamsulosin, Doxazosin; Antifungals: Ketoconazole, Itraconazole; Antibiotics: Erythromycin, Clarithromycin.

Missed dose

If you forget to take P-Force Plus as instructed, take it as soon as you remember. However, if it is almost time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and continue with your regular dosing schedule. Never increase your dosage without consulting your doctor.

Overdose

If you accidentally take too much P-Force Plus, seek immediate medical attention. Taking too much of this medicine can lead to severe side effects such as a prolonged and painful erection (priapism). Contact your doctor or visit a nearby hospital if you experience any discomfort or unusual symptoms.
