Common use

Eukroma Cream is a melasma cream that is used to treat the condition. It is a skin-lightening drug that helps to lighten darkish patches and spots on the skin (hyperpigmentation). It reduces the quantity of melanin, a skin-darkening pigment, in the skin. It aids in skin depigmentation that is reversible.

Dosage and direction

Eukroma Cream should only be used externally. Use it according to your doctor's instructions for dosage and duration. Before using, read the directions on the label. Apply the cream after cleaning and drying the afflicted area. Unless your hands are the affected region, wash your hands after applying.

Precautions

This medicine is not recommended during pregnancy and breastfeeding unless recommended by your doctor. Consult your doctor before using this medicine during pregnancy and breastfeeding.
Caution is advised in patients with pre-existing skin conditions like eczema, and psoriasis, or those with excessive exposure to sunlight and UV therapy.

Contraindications

Eukroma Cream is contraindicated in individuals who have known hypersensitivity to Hydroquinone or any other ingredients in the formulation. It should not be used on open wounds, irritated, or abraded skin.

Possible side effect

The majority of side effects are minor and will go away as your body adjusts to the medicine. If they don't go away or you're worried about them, see your doctor.
Adverse effects side effects of Eukroma cream: Erythema (skin redness); Dry skin; Skin burn; Skin urineling; Itching.

Drug interaction

This medicine can interact with peroxide agents (benzoyl peroxide and hydrogen peroxide) and irritating topicals (resorcinol, salicylic acid, or phenol).

Missed dose

If you forget to apply Eukroma Cream, apply it as soon as you remember. But avoid repeating the application if the time for the next scheduled dose is near.

Overdose

Overdose of Eukroma Cream is unlikely as it is intended for external use only. Using the prescribed dosage is recommended to avoid any side effects.

Storage

Store at room temperature between 59-77 degrees F (15-25 degrees C), away from light and moisture, kids and pets. Do not use after expiration term.
