Common use

Tentex Royal capsule is used for supporting men's reproductive health. Tentex Royal helps men improve sexual desire and performance as well as erectile function. It contains Tribulus Terrestris, Ashwagandha, and Mucuna Pruriens, which may boost energy, libido, physical performance, and mood. Tentex Royal can uses also: Relieve inflammation, muscle spasms, and pain; Neurological disorders; Liver toxicity; Cardiovascular disease; Diuresis; Oxidative stress; High levels of lipids; Blood disorders.

Dosage and direction

Take 2 capsules daily 1 hour before bedtime or follow as suggested by the health care professional. Use a glass full of water to administer the capsule and avoid other drinks such as alcohol because it may increase your risk of adverse reactions. While swallowing, you should not split, crush, or chew the capsule. To get a better effect from this product, it is advised to administer the pill every day before bed. Contact a healthcare professional if your condition does not improve or worsens.

This tablet contains natural ingredients. However, avoid using it if you have any sensitivities to its ingredients. Stop using the product and consult a healthcare professional if you have an allergic reaction.

Possible side effect

You may experience some adverse effects during treatment with Tentex Royal. However, if your body adjusts to the ingredients of this product, you may not experience any of the following adverse effects. Check below some examples: Flushing; Indigestion; Diarrhea; Vision changes (such as blurred vision); Drowsiness; Bloody nose; Headaches; Dizziness. If you notice any of the adverse reactions listed above, contact a healthcare provider.

Drug interaction

Other drugs may interact with Tentex Royal, including prescription and over-the-counter medicines, vitamins, and herbal products. Tell each of your health care providers about all medicines you use now and any medicine you start or stop using.

Missed dose

If you forget and miss a dose, you should administer it as soon as possible. In case it is almost time for the next dose, just skip the missed dose and continue the regular treatment as prescribed. However, it is not allowed to used an increased dose of Tentex Royal the next time to catch up because it may lead to severe side effects.

Overdose

You may rarely experience an overdose usually, due to an increased amount of capsules. Symptoms of overdose include severe dizziness, passing out, slow or shallow breathing, irregular heartbeat, and others. If you notice any of the symptoms listed above, interrupt using this product and get medical help right away.

Storage

It is recommended to store this product in dry and cool places away from direct sunlight. Don't keep Tentex Royal in the bathroom. Keep away these capsules from the reach of children and pets.
