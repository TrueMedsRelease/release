Common use

Cialis Black is high-power pills which are to treat erectile dysfunction in men. The pills affect a strong and vey long-term influence. Cialis Black contains a high dosage of Tadalafil as its active ingredient - Phosphodiesterase type 5 (PDE5) inhibitor.

Dosage and direction

You should take Cialis Black orally no more then once per day. Consult your doctor concerning an appropriate dosage.

Precautions

Surely consult your doctor before taking Cialis Black if you have following health problems: vision problems, heart problems, high or low blood pressure, kidney or liver disease, allergic reaction to tadalafil.

Contraindications

Do not take Cialis Black with nitrates and alphablockers. It could cause you to become dizzy, faint, or even have a heart attack.
The patients who are allergic reaction to tadalafil shouldn't take Cialis Black.
Avoid taking grapefruit juice, because it can create undesirable side effects.

Possible side effect

The most common side effects are: back pain, dizziness, flushing, headache, indigestion, muscle aches, stuffy or runny nose.
Consult your doctor immediately if you have the following side effects: allergic reactions like skin rash, itching or hives, swelling of the face, lips, or tongue, breathing problems, changes in hearing, chest pain, and irregular heartbeat.

Drug interaction

Do not take Cialis Black with such medications as: amyl nitrite; isosorbide dinitrate; isosorbide mononitrate; nitroglycerin; drugs for high blood pressure; drugs used for fungal or yeast infections, like fluconazole, itraconazole, ketoconazole, and voriconazol; drugs used for seizures like carbamazepine, phenytoin, and phenobarbital; antibiotics like clarithromycin, erythromycin, troleandomycin; medicines for prostate problems, rifabutin, rifampin or rifapentine.

Missed dose

You should follow the recommende dose to get the desired effect. Take only one dose per day (24 hours).

Overdose

Do not take Cialis Black more than was prescribed by your doctor. If you exceed your dose contact your doctor as soon as possible.

Storage

Store at room temperature between 15 and 30 degrees C (59 and 86 degrees F). Keep away from children and pets.
