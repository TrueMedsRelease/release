Common use

Kiwof Puppy Suspension is a medicine used to treat and control various intestinal worm infections in dogs and cats. This includes roundworms, hookworms, whipworms, and certain tapeworms. It paralyzes and kills the worms, helping remove them from the body and preventing their growth and reproduction.

Dosage and direction

Give Kiwof Puppy Suspension exactly as directed by your veterinarian, in the recommended dose and duration. It can usually be given with or without food, but administering it with food may help reduce stomach discomfort. Make sure your pet swallows the full dose, and repeat deworming as advised by the veterinarian to prevent reinfection.

Precautions

Use with caution in pregnant or lactating pets, and always follow the prescribed dose to avoid toxicity. Regular veterinary checkups and fecal examinations are important to ensure complete elimination of worms.

Contraindications

Do not use Kiwof Puppy Suspension in weak or severely ill animals unless advised by a veterinarian. Avoid giving this medicine along with other deworming or anti-parasitic products unless specifically recommended by your vet.

Possible side effect

Kiwof Puppy Suspension is generally well tolerated and does not usually cause any side effects. However, some pets may experience mild side effects such as vomiting, diarrhea, or loss of appetite. These effects are usually temporary and resolve on their own. If symptoms persist, worsen, or if your pet shows signs of an allergic reaction like swelling or difficulty breathing, contact your veterinarian immediately.

Drug interaction

Kiwof Puppy Suspension must not be used in combination with other deworming medications, particularly those containing piperazine.

Missed dose

Give the medicine as soon as you can, but skip the missed dose if it is almost time for your next dose. Do not give two doses at one time.

Overdose

Seek your veterinarian for emergency medical attention.

Storage

Store at room temperature away from moisture and heat. Keep out of reach of the children and pets.
