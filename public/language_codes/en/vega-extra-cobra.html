Common use

Vega Extra Cobra is a medication designed to address erectile dysfunction in men. It contains sildenafil citrate, a well-known and effective treatment that enhances blood flow to the penis, helping achieve and maintain an erection.

Dosage and direction

Patients looking to experience the full therapeutic benefits of Vega Extra Cobra are advised to consume the medication at least 30 - 45 minutes prior to engaging in any sexual activity. Patients taking Vega Extra Cobra are advised to not exceed the recommended one dose per 24-hour period.

Precautions

Before starting Vega Extra Cobra, discuss your complete medical history with your healthcare provider, including any other medications you are taking, to prevent harmful interactions and ensure the medication is appropriate for you.
Follow the prescribed dosage instructions carefully. Avoid taking more than the recommended dose to minimize the risk of side effects.
Limit alcohol consumption while using sildenafil, as excessive drinking can increase the risk of side effects. Also, avoid grapefruit and grapefruit juice, which can interact with sildenafil and increase its side effects.

Contraindications

Heart Conditions: If you have a history of heart disease, heart attack, stroke, or severe heart rhythm disorders, consult your healthcare provider before using this medication. Sexual activity can put extra strain on the heart, and sildenafil might exacerbate these conditions.
Nitrate Use: Do not use Vega Extra Cobra if you are taking any form of nitrate medication (e.g., nitroglycerin) for chest pain or angina. Combining sildenafil with nitrates can cause a dangerous drop in blood pressure.
Severe Liver or Kidney Disorders: If you have severe liver or kidney issues, inform your healthcare provider. These conditions can affect how sildenafil is metabolized and cleared from your body, potentially leading to an increased risk of side effects.
If you experience sudden vision loss or hearing loss, discontinue use and contact your healthcare provider. These are rare but serious side effects that require immediate attention.

Possible side effect

Vega Extra Cobra, like other sildenafil-based medications, can cause side effects. Some Potential side effects include: Headache; Flushing; Indigestion; Nasal Congestion; Dizziness; Priapism; Severe Allergic Reactions; Chest Pain.

Drug interaction

Vega Extra Cobra can interact with various drugs, potentially leading to serious health issues. Here are key drug interactions to be aware of: Nitrates; Alpha-Blockers; Other Erectile Dysfunction Medications; Antifungal Medications; HIV Protease Inhibitors; Antibiotics; Blood Pressure Medications.

Missed dose

Vega Extra Cobra is taken as needed rather than on a regular schedule. If you miss a dose, simply take it as needed before sexual activity. Do not take more than one dose within a 24-hour period.

Overdose

If you think you have used too much of this medicine seek emergency medical attention right away. The symptoms of overdose usually include chest pain, nausea, irregular heartbeat, and feeling light-headed or fainting.

Storage

Store your medicines at room temperature between 68-77 degrees F (20-25 degrees C), away from light and moisture. Do not store the drugs in the bathroom. Keep all drugs away from reach of children and pets.
