Common use

Neurobion Forte RF Injection is used to treat and prevent vitamin B-complex deficiencies. It supports nerve health and proper nerve function. The injection helps relieve tingling, numbness, and weakness. It is commonly used in neuropathy and nutritional deficiencies. The injection helps support nerve function and relieve deficiency-related symptoms.

Dosage and direction

Administer Neurobion Forte RF Injection as follows: The medicine is administered through deep intramuscular injection, usually in the gluteal region. Ensure the injection is only administered by a qualified professional. Intravenous administration is not advised due to potential adverse effects.
Your doctor will recommend the daily dose of Neurobion Forte RF Injection required for you. This dosage will be tailored to your needs, including the severity of your condition and your response to treatment.

Precautions

Due to its role in fetal neural development, Neurobion Forte RF Injection is often used as an adjunct supplement in pregnancy. However, it's best to consult your doctor before using this medicine during pregnancy.
If you have liver health issues, consult your doctor before using this medicine, as the liver plays a key role in metabolising the ingredients in the injection. Neurobion Forte RF Injection should be used with care if you have kidney problems. Consult your doctor before using this medicine if you have a history of kidney disorders.

Contraindications

If you know that you are allergic to any components of Neurobion Forte RF Injection, do not use this product.
Parkinson’s (Levodopa): Pyridoxine may reduce levodopa effect. Polycythemia Vera: May trigger symptoms early after B12 injection.

Possible side effect

Neurobion Forte RF Injection may cause side effects including pain, redness, or swelling at the injection site, nausea, vomiting, dizziness, headache, flushing, and allergic reactions such as rash or itching. Most side effects are temporary, but severe allergic reactions require immediate medical attention. Inform your doctor if any unusual symptoms persist.

Drug interaction

Neurobion Forte RF Injection can increase the risk of bleeding when used with anticoagulants like warfarin or antiplatelet medications such as clopidogrel. Neurobion Forte RF Injection may reduce the effectiveness of levodopa, a medication for Parkinson's disease.

Missed dose

If you've missed a dose of Neurobion Forte RF Injection, administer it as soon as you remember. If it's nearly time for your next dose, skip the missed one and continue with your regular schedule. Don't administer extra doses to make up for a missed one.

Overdose

An overdose of Neurobion Forte RF Injection might cause symptoms such as itching, headache, or flushing. If you've administered more than the prescribed dose, inform your doctor promptly.

Storage

Keep Neurobion Forte RF Injection at or below 25°C, ideally in a cool, dry place. Shield it from direct light and moisture. Store it in a place out of children's and pets reach.
