Common use

Ophthacare Eye Drops is used for eyestrain relief, redness and dry eyes. It contains natural plant extracts such as Madhu (Honey), and Satapatri (Rosa damascena or Damask Rose). These eye drops, carefully made with strong natural components are intended to relieve dryness and irritation while also offering comfort to your eyes. Furthermore, the Ophthacare Eye Drops alleviate the discomfort of a surface scrape by flushing out any remaining unsafe particles.

Dosage and direction

Put 2-3 drops in each eye at night for best results. Consult your doctor to prescribe the dosage as per your situation. Do not exceed the prescribed Ophthacare Eye Drops dosage.

Precautions

Pregnant or lactating women and people with any medical condition should consult a doctor before use.

Contraindications

Avoid using the product if you have an allergy to any of the ingredients. It is instructed not to combine with other liquids.
Avoid wearing contact lenses when using eye drops. Remove your contact lenses before applying Ophthacare eye drops, and then put them back in after 15 minutes.

Possible side effect

Ophthacare eye drops may produce transient blurred vision after being instilled in the eyes. As a result, it is suggested that you wait until your eyesight clears before driving or using machinery.

Drug interaction

It is advised that you wait at least 5 minutes after using Ophthacare eye drops before using additional eye drops. Please with your doctor before taking additional medications with Ophthacare eye drops.

Missed dose

If you miss a dose of Ophthacare eye drops, take it as soon as possible. However, if it is almost time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and go back to your regular schedule. Do not double the dose.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical attention.

Storage

Should not come in direct contact with the sun. Store the product safely in a cool and dry place. Keep the eyedrop away from children.
