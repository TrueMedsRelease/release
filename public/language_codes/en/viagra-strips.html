Common use

Viagra oral strips are used to treat erectile dysfunction (ED) in men, providing a fast-acting, discreet alternative to tablets that dissolves on the tongue. They help achieve and maintain an erection during sexual stimulation by increasing blood flow to the penis.

Dosage and direction

Use dry fingers to remove the strip from its sachet. Place the strip on the tongue and let it dissolve, which usually takes a few seconds. Do not swallow the strip whole; wait for it to melt. It is designed to be taken before sexual activity, and high-fat meals may delay the onset of action.

Precautions

Consult a doctor if you have had a heart attack, stroke, or severe heart failure within the last 6 months. Avoid it if you have severe heart problems, severe liver issues, low blood pressure, or hereditary eye diseases (e.g., retinitis pigmentosa). Caution is advised if you have to undergo surgery, or dental treatment.

Contraindications

Viagra Strips is contraindicated in patients with a history of hypersensitivity to Sildenafil. It is also contraindicated in patients with pulmonary veno-occlusive disease, stomach ulcer, heart disease, kidney disease, liver disease, patients who had a heart attack, heart failure, angina, irregular heartbeat, stroke, chest pain, high or low blood pressure, high cholesterol, bleeding disorder, blood circulation problems, blood cell problems such as sickle cell anemia, multiple myeloma, leukemia, eye disease or diabetes.

Possible side effect

Side effects of Viagra Strips: Headache; Heartburn; Diarrhea; Nausea; Flushing; Nosebleeds; Pain in muscles, arms, and legs.

Drug interaction

This drug should not be used with nitrates and recreational drugs called "poppers" containing amyl or butyl nitrite; alpha-blocker medications; other medications for treatments for ED, such as avanafil (Stendra), tadalafil (Cialis), or vardenafil (Levitra); high blood pressure medicines, etc. Consult your doctor or pharmacist for more details.

Missed dose

Viagra Strips is used as needed, so you are unlikely to be on a dosing schedule.

Overdose

In case you accidentally take an overdose of this medication and experience severe side effects or worsening of any of the symptoms, please consult your doctor. Strict adherence to the prescribed dose is essential.

Storage

Store at room temperature away from moisture and heat. Keep out of reach of the children.
