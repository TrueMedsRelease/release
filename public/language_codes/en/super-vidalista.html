Common use

Super Vidalista tablet is used to treat Erectile Dysfunction and Premature Ejaculation. These tablets contain Tadalafil and Dapoxetine, as active ingredients. Tadalafil is a phosphodiesterase (PDE-5) inhibitor, which basically works in Erectile Dysfunctioning by increasing blood flow to the penis by relaxing the muscle in penile blood vessels in men. Dapoxetine is a Selective Serotonin Reuptake Inhibitor which is used in treating premature ejaculation.

Dosage and direction

Super Vidalista dose should be taken at a fixed time as directed by your doctor. Swallow the tablet with water without chewing or crushing it. It is always advised to take it with food to avoid stomach upset. Generally, one tablet per day, one hour before the intercourse is enough in combating your health issue.

Precautions

Make sure that: You are not allergic to Tadalafil and Dapoxetine; You do not take alcohol; Your doctor knows about your Pre-existing diseases, surgeries, Medications, Food Habits, Herbal Supplements, vitamins, etc.
Tell your doctor if you: Are taking nitrates; Ever had a Heart attack, Stroke, Low blood pressure; Have severe Liver or Kidney disease; Are having other diseases, drugs or therapies.

Contraindications

You should avoid Super Vidalista if you: Have ever noticed any allergy or hypersensitivity to Tadalafil or Dapoxetine; Have any heart-related problem or blood pressure issue; Are a female or children.

Possible side effect

Common Super Vidalista side effects: Headache; Bloody Nose; Sleeplessness; Dizziness; Burning, Numbness, Tingling in the Arms and Feet; Prolonged and Painful Erection; Flushing; Indigestion; Diarrhea; Bloody and Cloudy Urine; Changes in Vision; Sensitivity to Light; Painful Urination.

Drug interaction

Super Vidalista may interact with other medicine or supplements you are taking and can produce some adverse effects or cause your drug not to work properly. Hence, you must tell your doctor if you are on any vitamins, herbal or other OTC drugs supplements. It interacts with the following medicines: Amlodipine; Carbamazepine; Clarithromycin; Telithromycin; Dexamethasone; Ketoconazole; Atazanavir, Ritonavir; Nitroglycerin; Nitric oxide.

Missed dose

A missed dose of Super Vidalista should be taken as soon as you remember. Do not double the dosage for next time if you missed once. Multiple doses at the same time can cause harmful effects.

Overdose

Contact your doctor or reach nearby medical facility if you are suspecting the overdose symptoms.

Storage

Store the Super Vidalista at room temperature in dark, clean and dry place. Keep away from children.
