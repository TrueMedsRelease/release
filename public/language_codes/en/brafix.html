Common use

Brafix is breast booster supplement, that helpful in development amp maintenance of breast. Brafix keep breast shape in natural and tightness intact.

Dosage and direction

Suggested dosage is 2 pills twice a day or as recommended by your healthcare professional.

Precautions

Consult with a healthcare professional before starting any new dietary supplement, including Brafix capsules.
Brafix capsules are not intended to replace medical procedures or treatments.

Contraindications

You should not use Brafix if you are allergic to any ingredient of the product: Kamal kakdi, Lotus root, Shatavari (Asparagus racemosus) and other.

Possible side effect

There are no known side effects, but if you experience any discomfort call your doctor for medical advice.

Drug interaction

Other drugs may affect Brafix, including prescription and over-the-counter medicines, vitamins, and herbal products. Tell your doctor about all other medicines you use.

Missed dose

Take the medicine as soon as you can, but skip the missed dose if it is almost time for your next dose. Do not take two doses at one time.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical attention.

Storage

Store at room temperature away from moisture and heat. Keep out of reach of the children.
