Common use

Tadalista Super Active is a medication primarily used to treat erectile dysfunction (ED) in men. It contains Tadalafil, a phosphodiesterase type 5 (PDE5) inhibitor, which enhances blood flow to the genital area, facilitating and maintaining an erection during sexual stimulation.

Dosage and direction

Taking Tadalista Super Active is simple. Swallow the tablet whole with a glass of water about 30 minutes before engaging in sexual activity. Avoid crushing or breaking the tablet for optimal effectiveness. The recommended dosage is one tablet per day. It’s crucial to follow your healthcare provider’s prescription for the correct dosage based on your individual health condition.

Precautions

Individuals with heart conditions or those taking nitrates should exercise caution when using Tadalista Super Active. Consult with your healthcare provider to discuss potential risks and benefits.

Contraindications

Liver Disease: People with existing liver conditions might find it difficult to process Tadalista Super Active. Doctors usually prescribe lower strengths to them.
Kidney Impairment: People with kidney problems need to be monitored to prevent further kidney damage. They are usually recommended low strength doses in the beginning.
Tadalista Super Active is not advised for women, and its effects on pregnant and breastfeeding women are not widely known.
Avoid medications like Nitrates or Alpha-blockers. They can often result in side effects like fainting or dizziness.
Avoid grapefruit juice and alcohol consumption while taking Tadalista Super Active, as they can have adverse effects.

Possible side effect

Tadalista Super Active may cause some mild side effects that might not need medical attention. Tadalista Super Active side effects include: Flushing; Headache; Dizziness; Stuffy nose; Muscle pain.
However, it is advised to contact your doctor in case any of these symptoms worsen.

Drug interaction

Inform your doctor about all medications you are taking, especially nitrates, to avoid potential interactions that may lead to adverse effects. It’s essential to follow your healthcare provider’s advice on medication interactions.
Never take Tadalista Super Active with any medication which contain nitrates for example ones to treat angina such as nitro-glycerine (in form of tablets, ointments, sprays, skin patches or pastes) isosorbide mononitrate and isosorbide dinitrate, or recreational drugs ("poppers") such as amyl nitrate or nitrite.

Missed dose

The medication is taken when needed. Do not exceed the recommended dose.

Overdose

In case of suspected overdose seek for urgent medical attention.

Storage

Store Tadalista Super Active in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep it out of reach of children.
