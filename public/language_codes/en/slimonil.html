Common use

Slimonil helps to lose weight in males by burning fat. The unique combination of herbs helps to get rid of excessive body weight. Medohar Guggulu, Vidang, Vijayasar, Punarnava, Rudrajata, and Pippalimul are the key ingredients of this medicine.

Dosage and direction

Take the capsule whole by mouth with a glass of water and avoid other beverages such as alcohol because it may lead to severe side effects. Do not split, chew, or break the capsule while swallowing. Your physician may increase the dosage or even change the medicine if you do not notice the desired result. To get the most benefit from Slimonil, you should use it regularly at the same time each day.

Precautions

Slimonil should be used with caution in the following cases: Stomach problems; If you are pregnant or breastfeeding women. Consul with you doctor before use.

Contraindications

This remedy should not be consumed on an empty stomach. Pregnant women are advised to consult their doctor before usage. The remedy is for patients above the age of 14 years.

Drug interaction

Other drugs may interact with Slimonil, including prescription and over-the-counter medicines, vitamins, and herbal products. Tell each of your health care providers about all medicines you use now and any medicine you start or stop using.

Missed dose

If you miss a dose of Slimonil capsule, take it as soon as possible. However, if it is almost time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and go back to your regular schedule. Do not double the dose.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical attention.

Storage

Store at room temperature away from moisture and heat. Keep away from children and pets.
