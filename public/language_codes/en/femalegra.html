Common use

Femalegra is an oral medication designed to help women who experience low sexual desire or Female Sexual Arousal Disorder (FSAD). It contains Sildenafil Citrate, which is the same active ingredient found in Viagra used for men. Femalegra works by increasing blood flow to the female genital area, which may enhance sensitivity, lubrication, and sexual pleasure.

Dosage and direction

Here are a few guidelines to keep in mind while taking this medicine:
Take this medication 30-60 minutes before any planned sexual activity
Avoid crushing or breaking the tablet and swallow the tablet as a whole
Do not take more than one dosage in a day for safe female sexual enhancement
You can take this tablet with or without food based on your preference

Precautions

Femalegra therapy comes with some safety restrictions. This drug should be used by women who have problems related to sex. Various health conditions are known to interfere with this medicine and make it even worse. It is therefore important to consider all the health conditions before getting on board with this medicine.
Some of the health conditions that are known to interfere with this drug as listed:
Hypertension
Heart Problems
Having Kidney Disorder
Diabetes
Retinitis Pigmentosa
Having a high cholesterol level

Contraindications

Liver Disease: There is very limited information about the effects of Sildenafil medications in individuals with severe liver disease. Thus, it is best to consult your doctor before using Femalegra.
Kidney Impairment: Femalegra is usually safe for individuals with kidney impairments. However, you should always inform your doctor so that they can modify the dosage according to the need.
Pregnant and breastfeeding women: Since there is very limited information on the safety of Sildenafil medications in pregnant or breastfeeding women, it is advised to avoid this medicine.
Avoid: It is best to avoid taking this medicine with alcohol or grapefruit juice. You should also avoid this medicine if you are allergic to Sildenafil.

Possible side effect

Femalegra, containing Sildenafil, is generally a safe medicine for promoting sexual wellness for women. However, some women may notice side effects.
Some of these Femalegra side effects are: Dizziness; Flushing; Headache; Nasal congestion; Indigestion; Heartburns.
Note: These side effects usually subside on their own and do not need medical intervention. Yet, if you are facing serious discomfort due to these side effects, it is best to consult your doctor immediately.

Drug interaction

Femalegra should not be taken with other medications like Alpha-blockers and Nitrates. It can result in serious side effects like dizziness and sudden low blood pressure.

Missed dose

If you miss a dose, take it as soon as you remember. But, if the next dose is near, skip the missed dose and resume the regular dosing regime.

Overdose

If you think you have used too much of this medicine seek emergency medical attention right away. The symptoms of overdose usually include chest pain, nausea, irregular heartbeat, and feeling light-headed or fainting.

Storage

Store your medicines at room temperature between 68-77 degrees F (20-25 degrees C) away from light and moisture. Do not store the drugs in the bathroom. Keep all drugs away from reach of children and pets.
