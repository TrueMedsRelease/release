Common use

Tastylia Strips - Tadalafil Orally Disintegrating Strips is an orally disintegrating strip drugs for treating erectile dysfunction (ED). Tastylia Strips provides an oral administration route and rapid absorption. You have to just keep the Oral Tastylia Strip on the tongue and allow it to dissolve on the tongue for few seconds. No need to use water. Benefits experienced in as little as about 10 minutes after sexual stimulation.

Dosage and direction

Tastylia Strips is a prescription-based Erectile dysfunction medicine that should be taken per your doctor's instructions. Below are the general guidelines for taking this medication effectively: Try to take the Tastylia strip half an hour before going into sexual intimacy; Keep the Orally Disintegrating Strip Tastylia on the tongue and allow it to dissolve on the tongue for few seconds; No need to use water; You can take Tastylia strip on an empty stomach or after a light meal; Do not take more than one dose in 24 hours.

Precautions

To make sure this medicine is safe for you, tell your doctor if you have ever had: heart disease or heart rhythm problems; a heart attack or stroke; angina (chest pain), high or low blood pressure; pulmonary hypertension (unless you are taking tadalafil for this); a condition called pulmonary veno-occlusive disease (narrowing of the veins that carry blood from the lungs to the heart); liver or kidney disease (or if you are on dialysis); vision loss, or retinitis pigmentosa (an inherited condition of the eye); a bleeding disorder; a blood cell disorder such as sickle cell anemia, multiple myeloma, or leukemia; a physical deformity of the penis (such as Peyronie's disease), or an erection lasting longer than 4 hours; a stomach ulcer; or health problems that make sexual activity unsafe.
Drinking alcohol with this medicine can cause a drop in blood pressure, leading to dizziness or fainting.
Grapefruit and grapefruit juice may interact with Tastylia Strips (tadalafil) and lead to unwanted side effects. Avoid the use of grapefruit products while taking this medicine.

Contraindications

You should not take Tastylia Strips if you are allergic to tadalafil.
Taking Tastylia Strips with certain other medicines can cause a sudden and serious decrease in blood pressure. Do not take this medicine if you also use: riociguat (to treat pulmonary arterial hypertension); or a nitrate drug (for chest pain or heart problems) such as nitroglycerin, isosorbide dinitrate, isosorbide mononitrate, or recreational drugs such as amyl nitrate or nitrite "poppers".

Possible side effect

Common Tastylia Strips side effects may include: headache; flushing (warmth, redness, or tingly feeling); nausea, upset stomach; stuffy nose; or muscle pain, back pain, pain in your arms, legs, or back.

Drug interaction

Tell your doctor about all your current medicines and any you start or stop using, especially: medicines to treat erectile dysfunction or pulmonary arterial hypertension; an antibiotic or antifungal medicine; antiviral medicine to treat hepatitis C or HIV/AIDS; drugs to treat high blood pressure or a prostate disorder; or seizure medicine.

Missed dose

Tastylia Strips is used as needed, you are not likely to be on a dosing schedule. Do not take extra medicine.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical attention.

Storage

Store at room temperature away from moisture and heat. Keep away from children.
