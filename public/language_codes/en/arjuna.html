Common use

Arjuna tablet is used for supporting normal cardiac function and promoting overall heart health. It helps regulate circulation, reduce the chances of clot formation and maintain a healthy lipid profile. This supplement is formulated with 250 mg of Arjuna (Terminalia arjuna) bark extract per tablet. Arjuna is known for its role in improving coronary artery blood flow, protecting heart muscles and enhancing cardiorespiratory endurance.

Dosage and direction

Take 1-2 tablets twice a day with water, ideally after meals. Consult an expert for the appropriate dose depending on your unique needs.

Precautions

Please inform your physician before consuming in the following situations: Pregnancy; Breastfeeding; Diabetes; Hypertension.

Contraindications

Do not use if you are allergic to any component of the Arjuna.
Not recommended for children under 14 years of age.

Possible side effect

High doses of Arjuna may cause drowsiness, dizziness, and confusion.
If you experience adverse Arjuna side effects on the liver when taking Arjuna, you must discontinue use and consult your healthcare provider immediately.
Arjuna side effects: Abdominal pain; Nausea or vomiting; Digestive problems such as gas, bloating, and diarrhoea; Skin rashes.

Drug interaction

May interact with certain medications, such as blood thinners. You should also avoid using other herbs or supplements containing compounds that could harm your liver.

Missed dose

If you miss a dose of Arjuna, take it as soon as possible. However, if it is almost time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and go back to your regular schedule. Do not double the dose.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical attention.

Storage

Store in a cool dry place away from direct sunlight. Keep out of reach of the children.
