Common use

Cenforce FM is usually used to treat female sexual problems (such as sensual disorders). This medicine should not be used when health conditions, relationship problems, mental or mood disorders, and others cause reduced sexual desire. This medicine should not be used in men. Sildenafil Citrate is the active ingredient of Cenforce FM.

Dosage and direction

The recommended dose per day (24 hours) is usually one tablet or as directed by your doctor. Take the tablet whole by mouth on an empty stomach with a glass full of water and avoid other beverages such as grapefruit juice and alcohol because it may increase the risk of side effects. However, if stomach upset or nausea occurs, it is recommended to administer the tablet with light food. Do not use this medicine more often and for longer than your doctor prescribes because it will not treat your health condition faster.

Precautions

Inform your healthcare professional if you have an allergy to the ingredients of the drug or if you have any other allergies. Additionally, share with your doctor your medical history. For example: Abuse of alcohol or drugs; Kidney or liver problems; Cancer; Hypotension (low blood pressure); Hypertension (high blood pressure).

Contraindications

Cenforce FM is contraindicated in patients with a history of hypersensitivity to Sildenafil. It is also contraindicated in patients with pulmonary veno-occlusive disease, stomach ulcer, heart disease, kidney disease, liver disease, patients who had a heart attack, heart failure, angina, irregular heartbeat, stroke, chest pain, high or low blood pressure, high cholesterol, bleeding disorder, blood circulation problems, blood cell problems such as sickle cell anemia, multiple myeloma, leukemia, eye disease or diabetes.

Possible side effect

Severe side effects. In rare cases, you may notice this category of side effects. If you are experiencing any of the following side effects, stop using Cenforce FM and consult a doctor at once: Breathing problems; Chest pain; Fainting; Hypotension; Stomach pain.
Cenforce FM common side effects. Usually, this category of adverse effects is not dangerous for the patients. It means that they can continue the treatment safely. However, tell your doctor if you experience any of the following side effects. Your physician may help you to prevent or avoid side effects: Weakness; Tiredness; Headaches; Nausea; Vomiting; Diarrhea; Dizziness; Sleepiness; Lightheadedness; Indigestion.

Drug interaction

Certain substances and drugs may interact with Cenforce FM. It means that other medicines may change how this medicine works by increasing the risk of side effects or decreasing its effectiveness. Inform your physician about all products that you are using before taking Sildenafil Citrate. For example herbal products, prescription drugs, nonprescription drugs, supplements, vitamins, and others. Check some examples: Loxapine; Lorazepam; Venlafaxine; Cetirizine; Warfarin; Alcohol; Ketoconazole; Hydrocodone; Codeine; Fluconazole; Verapamil; Diphenhydramine.

Missed dose

If you miss a dose of Cenforce FM, take it as soon as you remember. But, if the next dose is near, skip the missed dose and resume the regular dosing regime.

Overdose

In case you accidentally take an overdose of this medication and experience severe side effects or worsening of any of the symptoms, please consult your doctor. Strict adherence to the prescribed dose is essential.

Storage

You should store this medicine at room temperature away from direct sunlight and moisture. It is not allowed to keep Cenforce FM in the bathroom. Keep away from children and pets.
