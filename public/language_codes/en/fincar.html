Common use

Fincar tablet is used primarily to manage symptoms related to benign prostatic hyperplasia (BPH) in adult men. This condition, characterized by an enlarged prostate, is managed with this medicine which belongs to a group of medications known as 5Î±-reductase inhibitors. In addition to managing BPH, this medicine also aids in reducing the risk of acute urinary retention and decreases the need for surgery related to prostate enlargement. It works by mitigating symptoms such as difficulty or frequent urination by controlling the size of the prostate.

Dosage and direction

Swallow one tablet whole with a glass of water. Do not crush, break or chew the tablet. Try to take it at the same time every day, as consistency helps in long-term treatments.

Precautions

Caution is advised in older adults and patients with prostate cancer, depression and in patients with liver impairment.

Contraindications

Fincar is contraindicated in patients with hypersensitivity to Finasteride. It is also contraindicated in pregnant women, breastfeeding women, children and women with childbearing potential.

Possible side effect

Fincar tablet may cause the following common side effects: Decreased libido; Erectile dysfunction; Ejaculation disorder; Decreased ejaculate volume; Breast enlargement or tenderness (this could be swelling or discomfort in your chest area); Skin rash.

Drug interaction

Antifungals: Medicines such as ketoconazole may increase the levels of finasteride in your body, potentially enhancing its effects and side effects.
Alpha-adrenergic antagonists: Medicines like terazosin and tamsulosin, when taken with Fincar tablet, may increase the risk of low blood pressure or hypotension.

Missed dose

In case you forget to take your regular dose of Fincar tablet, take it as soon as you remember. If it's nearly time for the next dose, don't worry about the missed one and continue with your regular schedule. Don't increase your dose without consulting your doctor.

Overdose

If you accidentally take more Fincar tablets than directed, you might experience some common side effects in a more enhanced manner. You should inform your doctor if such a situation arises so they can guide you accordingly.

Storage

Store Fincar tablet at room temperature. Keep it in a tightly closed container to protect from light and moisture. Ensure the storage area is out of reach of children and pets.
