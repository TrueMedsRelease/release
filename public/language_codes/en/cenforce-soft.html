Common use

Cenforce Soft is a specially formulated medicine containing Sildenafil Citrate as its main ingredient. This medicine is used in the treatment of Erectile Dysfunction (ED) or Impotence in males. The Sildenafil Citrate in this pill belongs to the class of medicine known as Phosphodiesterase Type 5 or PDE-5 inhibitor.

Dosage and direction

Cenforce is a safe medicine to treat ED in men. However, you should follow certain guidelines to ensure the maximum effectiveness of this ED pill:
Take Cenforce 30-60 minutes before engaging in sexual activity with your partner.
Cenforce tablets can be ingested on an empty or full stomach.
Take the pill as a whole and avoid chewing, crushing, or breaking it.
Strictly avoid taking more than one Cenforce dosage to get relief from Erectile Dysfunction.

Precautions

With any drug, it is vital that you know what precautions are needed to be taken before having it. Here are the precautions that you need to be aware of, if you plan on taking this pill:
If you are below 18 years of age, you cannot take this drug.
If you suffer from heart problems, this medicine is not for you.
If you have eaten a high fat meal, the effects of Cenforce Soft would take longer to have effect.
If you are hypertensive, you should not take this medication.
If you have consumed alcohol, the effects of the drug could be less. Also, the drug would take a longer time to have effect.

Contraindications

Cenforce Soft is contraindicated in patients taking another medicine to treat impotence or using a nitrate drug for chest pain or heart problems.
This medicine should not be taken by women and children as well as in patients with a known hypersensitivity to any component of the tablet.

Possible side effect

Some of Cenforce Soft side effects are: Headache; Upset stomach or heartburn; Flushing; Back and muscle pain; Swelling in arms, legs, and hands; Nausea.
Most of these symptoms do not require medical consultations. However, if the side effects worsen or some other side effects arise, it is best to consult your doctor promptly.

Drug interaction

You should not take Cenforce Soft with other medications, such as Nitrates, Alpha-blockers, or other ED medications. Always consult a doctor about all medications you're taking to avoid interactions.

Missed dose

There is no need to worry about missing a dose of Cenforce Soft. It can be taken when needed, but always avoid taking double doses in a day.

Overdose

If you think you have used too much of this medicine seek emergency medical attention right away. The symptoms of overdose usually include chest pain, nausea, irregular heartbeat, and feeling light-headed or fainting.

Storage

You should store this medicine at room temperature in a cool, dry place away from sunlight and moisture. Keep it out of the reach of children and pets.
