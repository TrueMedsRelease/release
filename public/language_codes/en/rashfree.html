Common use

Rashfree cream is used for the effective management of diaper rashes and minor skin irritations in infants and adults. It contains Zinc Oxide and Benzalkonium Chloride, which work by forming a robust protective barrier on the skin to shield against moisture and irritants while providing antiseptic benefits. This soothing formula actively reduces redness and itching, hydrating the affected area to accelerate natural skin recovery and comfort.

Dosage and direction

Cleanse and dry the affected area thoroughly. Apply gently a thin layer of Rashfree cream to the irritated area. Reapply as needed, preferably after diaper changes or when required.

Precautions

There is insufficient data on the use of Rashfree during pregnancy or lactation. It is advised to consult a doctor before using this medicine if you are pregnant or breastfeeding.
Individuals with extensive skin damage or severe skin infections should exercise caution when using Rashfree due to potential increased absorption leading to systemic effects.

Contraindications

Rashfree is contraindicated in individuals with hypersensitivity to benzalkonium chloride, zinc oxide, or any other ingredients present in the formulation.
Rashfree is also contraindicated for application on large open wounds or severe burns.

Possible side effect

Side effects are rare but may include allergic reactions such as rash or swelling. Consult a doctor if such symptoms occur.

Drug interaction

Rashfree may interact with other topical products. Avoid using it concurrently with other creams, lotions, ointments, or medications on the same area of the skin unless instructed by a doctor.

Missed dose

If you miss a dose of Rashfree, apply it as soon as you remember. If it is almost time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and continue with your regular schedule. Do not apply a double dose to make up for a missed one.

Overdose

Overdose of Rashfree is unlikely as it is intended for external use only. Using the prescribed dosage is recommended to avoid any side effects.

Storage

Store at a temperature not exceeding 30°C and protect from moisture. Avoid freezing the product. For external use only, avoid contact with eyes or open cuts. Keep out of the reach of children and pets.
