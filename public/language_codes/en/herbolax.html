Common use

Herbolax tablet is used for alleviating constipation and preparing the bowel for radiographic procedures. It is formulated with a blend of natural ingredients that enhance intestinal motility and soften stools. Key ingredients include Chebulic Myrobalan (Haritaki) which is a well-regarded Ayurvedic laxative that helps stimulate intestinal movement and supports digestion and Trivruth (Ipomoea turpethum), known for its mild laxative effect that helps manage constipation. Other components like Haritaki Extract, Kasani Extract and Yashtimadhu Extract contribute to its carminative and laxative actions, helping with constipation and indigestion.

Dosage and direction

You must consult your physician to prescribe the dosage that best suits your condition. Do not overdose and stop consumption when your condition is taken care of.

Precautions

Consult your physician before use, especially if you are pregnant, breastfeeding, or have specific health conditions that require special medical care.

Contraindications

The product includes synthetic colours and preservatives (Methylparabates and Propylparabates) which may cause allergic reactions in sensitive individuals.

Possible side effect

Herbolax is generally well-tolerated and not known to have any significant side effects when taken as directed. However, if you experience any unusual symptoms, consult your physician.

Drug interaction

Other drugs may interact with Herbolax, including prescription and over-the-counter medicines, vitamins, and herbal products. Tell each of your health care providers about all medicines you use now and any medicine you start or stop using.

Missed dose

If you miss a dose of Herbolax, take it as soon as possible. However, if it is almost time for your next dose, skip the missed dose and go back to your regular schedule. Do not double the dose.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical attention.

Storage

Store in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight and heat. Keep out of the reach of children.
