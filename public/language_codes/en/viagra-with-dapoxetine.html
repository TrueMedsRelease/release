Common use

Viagra with Dapoxetine tablets is a medicine that helps adult men with two common sexual health concerns: premature ejaculation and weak erections. This medicine does not cure these conditions but helps manage their symptoms. It helps men achieve normal sexual function.

Dosage and direction

Take the medicine as directed by your doctor. Do not crush or chew the tablet. Do not take more than one tablet within 24 hours.

Precautions

Low blood pressure: People with low blood pressure should be cautious when using Viagra with Dapoxetine tablets.
Eye diseases or vision problems: Individuals with eye diseases or vision problems should consult their doctor before using this medication.
History of fainting, mania, or depression: Those with a history of fainting, mania, or depression are advised not to take Viagra with Dapoxetine.

Contraindications

You should not use Viagra with Dapoxetine if you are allergic to Sildenafil or Dapoxetine.
Severe heart or liver problems: Individuals with severe heart or liver problems should avoid using Viagra with Dapoxetine due to potential risks.
Recent history of heart attack or stroke: If you have recently experienced a heart attack or stroke, this medication is not recommended for you.

Possible side effect

When taking Viagra with Dapoxetine, there are some possible side effects you should be aware of: Dizziness; Headache; Nausea; Stuffy nose; Facial flushing; Indigestion; Dry mouth; Vision problems (You may have changes in your vision, such as blurred vision or increased sensitivity to light).

Drug interaction

Do not take Viagra with Dapoxetine with similar medications such as sildenafil (Viagra), avanafil (Stendra), tadalafil (Cialis) or vardenafil (Levitra). Tell your doctor about all other medications you use for erectile dysfunction.
Avoid using the medicine with anti-depressants as it may increase the risk of side effects. Combining Viagra with Dapoxetine with nitrates can lead to a dangerous drop in blood pressure. Certain antibiotics can interact with the medicine, so it is important to inform your doctor about any ongoing antibiotic treatment. Some painkillers may interfere with the effectiveness of Viagra with Dapoxetine tablets.

Missed dose

You are unlikely to miss a dose if you are using it before sex as this medicine is taken when needed.

Overdose

If you accidentally take too much of the medicine, seek immediate medical attention. Common side effects of overdosing may include dizziness, headache, nausea, and vision problems.

Storage

Store tablets in a cool and dry place. Protect the medicine from direct sunlight, moisture, and excessive heat. Keep the product out of reach and sight of children.
