Common use

Bestina is a natural and herbal product that can be used for proper growth and development of breast region. Bestina aids in the toning of mammary gland muscles, improving shape and size.

Dosage and direction

Take 1-2 Bestina Ayurvedic capsules with a glass of warm water after breakfast or as directed by your healthcare professional. Continue regular usage for the recommended duration to achieve optimal results. Maintain a healthy lifestyle with a balanced diet and regular exercise for overall well-being.

Precautions

Consult with a healthcare professional before starting any new dietary supplement, including Bestina Ayurvedic capsules.
Bestina capsules are not intended to replace medical procedures or treatments.

Contraindications

You should not use Bestina if you are allergic to Shatavari (Asparagus racemosus) or any other ingredient of the product (Vidarikand (Pueraria tuberosa), Lodhra (Symplocos racemosa), Nagbala (Grewia hirsuta) or Ashwagandha (Withania somnifera).

Possible side effect

There are no known side effects, but if you experience any discomfort call your doctor for medical advice.

Drug interaction

Other drugs may affect Bestina, including prescription and over-the-counter medicines, vitamins, and herbal products. Tell your doctor about all other medicines you use.

Missed dose

Take the medicine as soon as you can, but skip the missed dose if it is almost time for your next dose. Do not take two doses at one time.

Overdose

Seek emergency medical attention.

Storage

Store at room temperature away from moisture and heat. Keep out of reach of the children.
