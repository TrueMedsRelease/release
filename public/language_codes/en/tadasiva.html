Common use

Tadasiva is a powerful drug that helps men cope with impotence and premature ejaculation. Tadasiva, also known as tadalafil with sildenafil, is the first ED drug of its kind that has helped men get the most out of their sexual activity. Tadalafil and Sildenafil Citrate belong to a class of drugs called phosphodiesterase inhibitors (PDE-5). It is known that they help men cope with a decrease in blood flow to the male reproductive organ, making it rigid and hard so that a man can have sex.

Dosage and direction

When the doctors prescribe you to use the Tadasiva it will generally be on a course. You can use the medicine daily if it is prescribed by the doctors or else take the medicines only when you need it. Take the subsequent pill only after a time lag of 24 hours.
Take the pills with water and swallow a pill whole. Do not crushing or chewing the pill using your teeth. If you have some swallowing problems then speak with the doctors.

Precautions

Using substances like Tadalafil or Sildenafil in excess amounts can lead you to overdose. Patients must also monitor any type of side effect and report them to the doctors. if you are using any type of medicines now do not forget to carry one sample of the drug including the back end information for the doctors to read if it has any contraindication chances.

Contraindications

Since Tadasiva is a very powerful complex drug that combines the effect of Sildenafil and Tadalafil at the same time, treatment should be coordinated with a doctor. Tablets are not recommended for men with serious kidney and liver diseases, as well as those who take nitrates and alpha-blockers on a regular basis. In case of overdose, headache, dizziness, dyspepsia, visual disturbances may occur. If such symptoms appear, it is necessary to consult a specialist to adjust the individual dosage.
Sexual activity has a potential risk for patients with cardiovascular diseases. Therefore, treatment of erectile dysfunction, including with the use of the drug Tadasiva, should not be carried out in men with such heart diseases in which sexual activity is not recommended.

Possible side effect

Like any other ED drugs, Tadasiva can cause side effects. Tadasiva side effects may appear: chest pain; dizziness; nasal congestion; priapism; weakness; drowsiness.
If the erection lasts more than 4 hours contact with doctor.

Drug interaction

This drug should not be used with nitrates and recreational drugs called "poppers" containing amyl or butyl nitrite; alpha-blocker medications; other medications for impotence; high blood pressure medicines.

Overdose

If you think you have used too much of this medicine seek emergency medical attention right away. The symptoms of overdose usually include chest pain, nausea, irregular heartbeat, and feeling light-headed or fainting.

Storage

Store Tadasiva at a temperature of 68 to 77 degrees F (20 to 25 degrees C). Keep away from heat, moisture and light sources. Keep out of the reach of children and pets.
