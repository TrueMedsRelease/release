Common use

Cephadex capsule is a medicine that is used to manage various bacterial infections in the body. Cephadex is commonly used to manage infections of the respiratory tract, skin and soft tissues, urinary tract, and ear. It can also be prescribed for preventing certain types of infections before surgeries.

Dosage and direction

Take the medicine exactly as prescribed by your doctor. Swallow the tablet whole with a glass of water. You may take the medicine with or without food. Do not stop taking the medicine even if you start feeling better unless advised by your doctor.

Precautions

If you are pregnant or planning a pregnancy, please consult your doctor before taking Cephadex capsule.
Cephadex passes into breast milk, so it is important to consult your doctor before breastfeeding while taking this medicine.
If you have liver disease or any liver-related concerns, please inform your doctor before starting Cephadex capsule.
Kidney disease: Cephadex capsule may require dosage adjustments in patients with kidney problems.
Stomach or intestinal disease: Cephadex capsule may worsen certain gastrointestinal conditions.

Contraindications

Cephadex is contraindicated in patients with known hypersensitivity to cephalosporin antibiotics. Also, it should not be administered to patients with a history of severe allergic reactions to similar drugs like penicillin.

Possible side effect

Side effects of Cephadex: Diarrhea; Nausea and vomiting; Abdominal pain; Skin rash; Headache.

Drug interaction

Medication categories and specific examples of medications that can interact with Cephadex include: Blood thinners: Warfarin, heparin; Diuretics: Furosemide, hydrochlorothiazide; Probenecid; Methotrexate.

Missed dose

If you forget to take a dose of Cephadex, take it as soon as you remember. If it's almost time for your next dose, skip the missed one and resume your regular dosing schedule. Never increase your dosage without consulting your doctor.

Overdose

If you accidentally take too much Cephadex capsule, seek immediate medical attention.

Storage

Store Cephadex capsule at room temperature. Keep the medicine in a dry place away from moisture. Protect it from direct sunlight and light exposure. Ensure that the medicine is out of reach of children.
