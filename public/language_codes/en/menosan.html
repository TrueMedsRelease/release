Common use

Menosan tablet is used managing menopausal symptoms like hot flashes and mood swings. It is an herbal formulation designed to support reproductive health in women. It is formulated with natural ingredients known for their beneficial effects on hormonal balance, mood stabilization and overall well-being during the menopausal transition.

Dosage and direction

Take 1-2 Menosan Tablets twice daily after meals or as directed by a healthcare professional.

Precautions

Menosan is formulated with natural herbal ingredients. There are no special restrictions, consult your doctor.

Contraindications

You should not take Menosan if you are allergic to any ingredients in the tablets or if you are planning a pregnancy or breastfeeding.

Possible side effect

Menosan tablets are generally well-tolerated. However, some individuals may experience mild gastrointestinal discomfort. Consult a healthcare provider if you experience any adverse effects.

Drug interaction

Sometimes it is not safe to use certain medicines at the same time. Some drugs can affect your blood levels of other drugs you use, which may increase side effects or make the medicines less effective. Tell your doctor about all your current medicines.
